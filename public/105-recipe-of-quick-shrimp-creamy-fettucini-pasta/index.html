The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Creamy Fettucini Pasta:
  1. Get Half package of Fettucinni Pasta
  2. Take 100-150 gr shrimps (I prefer bigger shrimp)
  3. Take 50 ml cooking cream
  4. Take 20 gr Margarine
  5. Prepare 1 large red onions
  6. Prepare Parmesan Cheese (as desired)
  7. Prepare Salt and Black Pepper
  8. Get Parsley for garnish
Instructions to make Shrimp Creamy Fettucini Pasta:
  1. Boil the fettucini for 10 min (or until al dente) add some salt and oil to the water. Strain and put aside a little bit of pasta water
  2. In other pan, Melt the margarine in the pan, add chopped red onions. After fragrant add shrimp until change color (don’t overcooked the shrimp)
  3. Add some cooking cream and parmesan cheese. Season with salt and black pepper
  4. Add the fetucinni to the sauce mixture and also pour in some pasta water (works like magic! 🤤)
  5. Give another taste and add seasoning. Garnish with some chopped parsley

Steps to make Shrimp and squid salad:
  1. Season the shrimps and squid (cut into rings) with salt, pepper for an hour
  2. Panfry the squid and shrimps until just cooked
  3. Keep the shrimp and squid in the fridge to keep cool
  4. Quarter the cherry tomatoes, cut the olive into rings
  5. Mix all ingredients with extra virgin olive oil, salt, pepper, garnish with some fresh basil leaves, enjoy😋

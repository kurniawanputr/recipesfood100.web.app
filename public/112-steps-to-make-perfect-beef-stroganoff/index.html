The ingredients needed to make Beef stroganoff:
  1. Take 2 boxes hamburger helper stroganoff
  2. Take 1 can drained spinage
  3. Prepare 2 rolls of ground meat
  4. Prepare 2 cups breadcrumbs
  5. Prepare 2 eggs

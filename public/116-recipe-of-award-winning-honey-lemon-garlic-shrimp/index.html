The ingredients needed to make Honey Lemon Garlic Shrimp:
  1. Get 1/2 kilo prawns
  2. Make ready 6 gloves minced garlic
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup turmeric shredded
  4. Take 1/4 cup butter
  5. Prepare 5 tablespoons honey
  6. Make ready 1 whole lemon juice
  7. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper
  8. Take Chopped Parsley for garnishing
Steps to make Honey Lemon Garlic Shrimp:
  1. Melt butter in a pan add garlic and turmeric then add shrimp
  2. Salt and pepper and add honey
  3. Add lemon juice and garnish with chopped parsley

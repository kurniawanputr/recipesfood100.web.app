The ingredients needed to make Beefy Bean Tacos:
  1. Get 1 tb ground beef
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp minced garlic
  3. Take 1/2 cup chopped onion
  4. Get 2 tsp salt
  5. Take 1 tsp black pepper
  6. Take 1 cup low sodium chicken broth
  7. Make ready 6 taco shells
  8. Make ready 2 cup refried beans
  9. Take 2 tbsp taco seasoning
  10. Get 3 tbsp taco sauce
  11. Prepare small diced tomatoes
  12. Take shredded cheese(of your choice)
  13. Make ready guacmole
  14. Get sour cream
Steps to make Beefy Bean Tacos:
  1. Heat some oil in a skillet pan and saute chopped onion and garlic.
  2. Once the onions are lightly browned, add the ground beef.
  3. Mix in the taco seasoning and salt and cook the beef until lightly browned.
  4. Now add the chicken broth and cook on simmer until the mixture is thick.
  5. Add the cooked bean into the beef mixture and mix well.
  6. Keep the taco mixture aside to cool.
  7. Spoon in some guacmole into the taco shells,followed by the beef bean filling,taco sauce,chopped tomato, sour cream and shredded cheese
  8. Make sure the tacos are not soggy.
  9. Serve the tacos fresh and you can reserve the filling for later use.
  10. Optional- You can melt the cheese on tacos and then add sour cream..
  11. Homemade Taco Seasoning:1 tsp each Paprika,cayenne and cumin powder,1/2tsp each blackpepper, salt and oregano.Combine everything & you are done!Store in an airtight container.

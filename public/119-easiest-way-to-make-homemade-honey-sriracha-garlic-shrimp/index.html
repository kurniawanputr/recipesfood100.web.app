The ingredients needed to make Honey Sriracha Garlic Shrimp:
  1. Take 1 pound jumbo shrimp shelled raw
  2. Get 1/2 cup honey
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup tamari soy sauce
  4. Take 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  5. Make ready 1 teaspoon cracked black pepper
  6. Make ready 1 tablespoon Sriracha sauce
  7. Get 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  8. Get 2 tablespoons seasoned rice vinegar
  9. Get Chilli olive oil and butter as needed for sautéing
  10. Take 4 Cloves garlic minced
  11. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon Sriracha seasoning
  12. Prepare 1 tablespoon cold butter cut into pieces
  13. Get 2 chopped green onions
Steps to make Honey Sriracha Garlic Shrimp:
  1. In a bowl, whisk together all ingredients except butter, oil, green onions and shrimp. Remove 1/4 cup of mixture to marinate the shrimp in. Save remaining sauce.Marinate shrimp for at least 15 minutes and up to an hour. 1. Heat chilli oil and butter in a skillet until hot. Add marinated shrimp and discard the marinate. Sauté shrimp until pink and just cooked through. Remove shrimp to a plate. Add reserved marinade to the pan used to cook the shrimp and cook until reduced by half and slightly thickened. Add the cold butter, turn off the heat and whisk until blended. Pour over shrimp, add onions, and serve with rice.

The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Scampi:
  1. Get 8 oz shrimp
  2. Prepare 2 tsp mirepoix base
  3. Take 3 handfuls baby spinach (chopped)
  4. Make ready 4 oz light cream
  5. Prepare 5 oz spaghetti
  6. Get 1 shallot (minced)
  7. Get 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  8. Get 1 oz grated Parmesan

Steps to make Shrimp Scampi:
  1. Pat shrimp dry and season with salt and pepper. Then cook the pasta with salt 8-10 mins (make sure to reserve 1/2 cup of that starchy pasta water when done).
  2. Cook shrimp on medium high 2 mins on each side while pasta is boiling. When done, add garlic and shallot and continue cooking for about a minute. Then add spinach until wilted. Lower heat to medium.
  3. Add cream, parmesan, base, and pasta water. Cook until desired thickness is reached. Add pasta and done.

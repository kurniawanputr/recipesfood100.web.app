The ingredients needed to make Beefy Garlic Tomato Sauce:
  1. Prepare 28 oz can crushed tomatoes
  2. Get 1/2 cup tomato juice
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp tomato paste
  4. Prepare 1/2 lb ground beef
  5. Take 1/2 lb spicy ground sausage
  6. Take 4 cloves minced garlic
  7. Get 1 small diced onion
  8. Get 1 tbsp dried oregano
  9. Make ready 1 tsp garlic powder
  10. Prepare 1 tsp sugar
  11. Prepare 1 tsp onion powder
  12. Prepare Salt and pepper
Steps to make Beefy Garlic Tomato Sauce:
  1. In a large pan brown your beef and sausage together seasoning with salt and pepper as it cooks. Drain and remove from pan.
  2. Cook your onion in the same pan, when it gets soft add the garlic and cook for another minute or two. Add in all other ingredients and bring to a simmer.
  3. Add your meat back to the sauce and simmer untill needed. If sauce gets too thick, add a bit more tomato juice.

The ingredients needed to make Crispy Prawn:
  1. Take Fresh Prawn peel the skin, left the tail on and deveined
  2. Make ready Bread Crumbs
  3. Take Flour (all purpose)
  4. Make ready 1 Egg
  5. Make ready to taste Pepper
  6. Take to taste Salt
  7. Get to taste Garlic powder
  8. Get Vegetable oil
Instructions to make Crispy Prawn:
  1. Place the prawn in the bowl and marinade with salt,pepper and garlic powder. Set aside
  2. In another bowl beat the egg.
  3. Place the flour in a plate and bread crumbs in another plate.
  4. Heat oil in the frying pan. Dip each prawn into the flour, then into the egg, and finally into the bread crumbs to coat.Â And deep fry them until golden brown on each side.

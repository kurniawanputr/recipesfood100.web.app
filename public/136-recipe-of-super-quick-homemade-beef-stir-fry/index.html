The ingredients needed to make Beef Stir Fry:
  1. Make ready 600 g beef
  2. Make ready 1 clove garlic
  3. Get 1 Tsp soy sauce
  4. Get 1 tp hoisin sauce (or oyster sauce)
  5. Get 1 carrot
  6. Take 1 onion
  7. Get 1/2 capsicum
  8. Take 1 cup beans
Instructions to make Beef Stir Fry:
  1. Slice meat against the grain. Marinate in soy sauce, hoisin and chopped garlic.
  2. Slice up veggies
  3. Heat up water. Put noodles in boiling water for 10 min. Break noodles up as they cook.
  4. Heat up wok and place vegetables gradually (High heat). Remove from wok when cooked
  5. Add more oil to wok and then meat. Cook half at a time.

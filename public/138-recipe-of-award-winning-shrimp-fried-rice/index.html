The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Fried Rice:
  1. Make ready vegetable oil
  2. Take plates cooked white rice (leftover rice, put in the refrigerator for a night, is recommended)
  3. Make ready shrimps, peeled and deveined
  4. Get frozen peas
  5. Prepare frozen corn
  6. Make ready medium carrot, small diced
  7. Prepare garlic, minced
  8. Get soy sauce
  9. Make ready salt or to taste
  10. Prepare white pepper powder or to taste
  11. Make ready green onions, thinly sliced
  12. Prepare sesame oil

The ingredients needed to make Cheesy and Beefy:
  1. Get 2 tablespoons File
  2. Take 1 pound ground sirloin
  3. Prepare 1/2 pound sausage
  4. Get 1 small onion
  5. Take 1 tablespoon yellow rice spices I used Vigo brand
  6. Get 1 teaspoon Pink Himalayan salt
  7. Make ready 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  8. Take 15 ounce can beef broth
  9. Get 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  10. Get 8 ounces cubed Colby jack cheese
  11. Get 1/2 cup gram flour / chickpea flour
  12. Take 1 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  13. Make ready 1/2 cup diced mushrooms

Instructions to make Cheesy and Beefy:
  1. Brown the sausage and ground sirloin add salt, pepper, and yellow rice seasonings. Add mushroom and onions.
  2. Make a crater in the center and add parmesan cheese. Stir well adding all the remaining ingredients.
  3. Cook till thickened up.
  4. Let rest. Serve I hope you enjoy!

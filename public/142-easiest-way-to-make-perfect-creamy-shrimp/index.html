The ingredients needed to make Creamy Shrimp:
  1. Take 3 tbsp Mayonnaise (kewpi mayo if available)
  2. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp Honey
  3. Prepare 1 pc Lemon
  4. Make ready 1/2 tbsp Condensed milk
  5. Make ready Salt
  6. Prepare Shrimps (deveined, peeled and tail removed)
  7. Prepare Tempura powder
  8. Make ready Cold water
  9. Get Cooking Oil
Steps to make Creamy Shrimp:
  1. Combine the mayonnaise, honey, zest of lemon, condensed milk lemon juice and a little salt. Mix well and set aside.
  2. Add the the tempura powder into the shrimps with 1/2 cup cold water. Mix well.
  3. Heat pan with cooking oil and deep fry the coated shrimps.
  4. Add the cooked shrimps into the mayo mixture and mix well.

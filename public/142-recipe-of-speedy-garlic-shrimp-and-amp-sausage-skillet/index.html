The ingredients needed to make Garlic shrimp & sausage skillet:
  1. Get 1 bag raw shrimp
  2. Take 1 package hot sausage
  3. Make ready 1/2 red bell pepper
  4. Take 1/2 red onion
  5. Take Olive oil
  6. Make ready 2 tbs minced garlic
  7. Take 2 tbs garlic powder
  8. Prepare 1 tbs garlic salt

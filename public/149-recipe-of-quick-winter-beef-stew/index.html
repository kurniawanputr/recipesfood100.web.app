The ingredients needed to make Winter Beef Stew:
  1. Make ready 1.5 lbs beef stew meat
  2. Take 1.5 lbs potatoes
  3. Prepare 6 cut up carrots
  4. Make ready 1 onion chopped
  5. Make ready 3 celery ribs chopped
  6. Get 4 tbsp flour
  7. Take 3 tbsp Olive oil
  8. Prepare 1 can diced tomatoes
  9. Prepare 1 can beef broth
  10. Make ready Salt and pepper
Steps to make Winter Beef Stew:
  1. Put potatoes, carrots, onion and celery in crockpot. Brown meat using flour and olive oil. Put over vegetables.
  2. Mix broth, tomatoes salt and pepper. Pour over meat.
  3. Cook on low for 8 hours on low.

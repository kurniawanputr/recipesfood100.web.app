The ingredients needed to make Celebration Pasta with Shrimp:
  1. Get 3 bags frozen jumbo shrimp
  2. Make ready 1 package linguine
  3. Get 2-3 shallots chopped
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp ghee
  5. Take 2 tbsp chopped garlic
  6. Prepare 3 cups fresh spinach
  7. Make ready 2 Roma tomatoes chopped
  8. Take 1/2 cup white wine or cooking wine
  9. Make ready 3/4 cup half and half
  10. Take 1 tbsp cornstarch
  11. Make ready 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions to make Celebration Pasta with Shrimp:
  1. Begin by prepping your ingredients once the pan gets hot you’ll be adding in. Make sure the shrimp is dried well and seasoned with salt and pepper. Chop shallots well and the artichoke hearts will be soft just drain and slice them up
  2. After ghee heats up add in shallots and garlic paste. Sauté for about 2 minutes then add in shrimp continually stir
  3. Squeeze the juice of one lemon over the shrimp when they are almost done. Remove shrimp set aside then add your white wine to remaining liquid.
  4. Add in chopped tomatoes, artichokes, and spinach. Let the spinach wilt down
  5. Take the half and half and stir in cornstarch this will thicken the sauce. Add this flurry in quickly you want it to thicken in the sauce. When the sauce begins to thicken remove from heat add in pasta then the parmesan cheese.

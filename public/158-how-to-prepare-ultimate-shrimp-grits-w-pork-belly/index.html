The ingredients needed to make Shrimp + Grits w/ Pork Belly:
  1. Make ready 2 lbs wild 60-70 shrimp (medium sized)
  2. Take 4 jalepenos, de-seeded and roughly chopped
  3. Take 4 oz high quality bacon or pork belly, chopped
  4. Prepare 1/2 Vidalia onion, chopped
  5. Take 1 lemon, juice
  6. Make ready 1/4 cup cornstarch
  7. Prepare 2 cloves garlic, minced
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup Valentina hot sauce or to taste
  9. Get 4 tbsp butter (unsalted)
  10. Get 1/4 cup water
  11. Take 1 tsp salt and cracked pepper
  12. Get 4 scallions, sliced, reserve extra for garnish
  13. Make ready 1/4 cup chopped parsley (for garnish)
  14. Make ready For the Grits
  15. Make ready 1 cup geechie boy grits (or wtv brand)
  16. Make ready 4 cups water
  17. Take 2 tbsp butter (unsalted)
  18. Take 1/4 cup cream cheese
  19. Take 1 tsp salt

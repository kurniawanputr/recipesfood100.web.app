The ingredients needed to make Beef Roll:
  1. Prepare 250 g beef slices
  2. Take 3 pcs potato
  3. Prepare Sauce:
  4. Prepare oyster sauce
  5. Take black pepper
  6. Take flour
  7. Make ready water
Steps to make Beef Roll:
  1. Boil potato until half done. Then cut into wedges.
  2. Let it cool and cut into wedges. Then lay flat the beef and roll.
  3. Heat pan and add oil. Fry in high heat or the beef changes color.
  4. Then pour the sauce simmer a while then serve.

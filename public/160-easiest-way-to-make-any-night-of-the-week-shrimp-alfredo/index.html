The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Alfredo:
  1. Prepare 1 (12 oz.) bag of frozen thawed peeled and deveined shrimp
  2. Take 3 tablespoons butter
  3. Get 3 tablespoons olive oil
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup diced raw garlic
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup half and half
  6. Make ready 1 cup chicken stock
  7. Make ready 1/4 cup white dry wine
  8. Make ready 1/2 sliced lemon juice
  9. Make ready Pinch salt and pepper
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup flour
  11. Take 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  12. Prepare Cooked and drained fettuccini
Instructions to make Shrimp Alfredo:
  1. Pinch tails off shrimp and discard.
  2. Combine olive oil and butter in a fry pan with garlic and sauté garlic on medium heat. Add shrimp after 3 minutes and cook until shrimp is pink. Remove shrimp from pan with slotted spoon.
  3. Add flour and combine with remaining oil in the pan with a whisk. Add chicken stock, half and half, wine, lemon and cheese and continue to stir over medium heat for 2-3 minutes. Once sauce thickens, return shrimp to pan.
  4. Add cooked fettuccine to shrimp Alfredo sauce and use liquid from noodles to thin the sauce to desired consistency.

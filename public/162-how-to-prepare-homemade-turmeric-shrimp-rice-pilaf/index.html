The ingredients needed to make Turmeric Shrimp Rice Pilaf:
  1. Take 3 lbs white shrimp peeled
  2. Get 1 red bell pepper chopped
  3. Get 1 orange bell pepper chopped
  4. Prepare 1 yellow bell pepper chopped
  5. Take 3 cloves garlic minced
  6. Make ready Turmeric
  7. Make ready 1 pound no. 65 Orzo pasta
  8. Get Olive oil
  9. Prepare Butter
  10. Prepare Water
  11. Prepare 4 chicken bouillon cubes
  12. Get Garlic salt
  13. Get Cracked black pepper
  14. Make ready Juice of 2 lemons
  15. Take 2 drops Liquid shrimp and crab boil
Instructions to make Turmeric Shrimp Rice Pilaf:
  1. Use Olive oil and butter to saute orzo pasta in a pot on medium heat
  2. Next add onion and garlic and continue to stir
  3. Then add the bell peppers and continue to stir
  4. Add water and chicken bouillon stirring well to melt the cubes then add garlic salt and pepper, lemon juice and liquid crab boil
  5. Coat shrimp with turmeric and add to mixture stirring well, turn heat off and place the lid on pot to let the shrimp steam

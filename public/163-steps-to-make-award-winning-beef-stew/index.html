The ingredients needed to make Beef Stew:
  1. Take 1 kg beef
  2. Get 3 medium onions
  3. Get 3 medium tomatoes
  4. Prepare 10-15 sabut lal mirch
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp sabut sukha dhaniya
  6. Get 1 tbsp zeera
  7. Take 2 tbsp sabut garam masala
  8. Make ready 1 lehsan
  9. Take 2 inches adrak
  10. Take 1 cup oil
  11. Get 4-5 sabut green chilli
  12. Make ready Namak
Instructions to make Beef Stew:
  1. Cooker m gosht or tamam masaly or pyaz tmator adrak lehsan kat kar dal dein sath hi 3cups pani dal kar 20mins ke liye pressure lagadein
  2. Without pressure cooker b same method sy pkana hai bus 1.5hour ya gosht galny tak low heat par paka lein
  3. 20mins baad flame off kar ke cooker ki steam nikalny dein phir open krein
  4. Gosht gal gya ho to excess water dry kar lein or oil dal kar bhonai kar lein
  5. Stew ready hai

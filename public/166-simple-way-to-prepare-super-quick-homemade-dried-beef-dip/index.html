The ingredients needed to make Dried Beef Dip:
  1. Make ready 1 Cream Cheese (8 oz)
  2. Take 1/2 pt. Sour Cream (8)
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp. Dill Weed
  4. Take 1/2 tsp. Garlic Powder
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp. Basil Leaves- ground
  6. Get 1/2 pig. Dried Italian dressing
  7. Prepare to taste Green Onion
  8. Make ready 1 small glass dried Beef
Instructions to make Dried Beef Dip:
  1. Mix together first 6 ingredients
  2. Dice the green onions and dried Beef and add to mixture
  3. Mix well and set in fridge to chill.
  4. Serve with crackers, wheat thins etc.

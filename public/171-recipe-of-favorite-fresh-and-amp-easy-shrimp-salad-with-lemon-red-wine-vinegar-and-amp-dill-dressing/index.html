The ingredients needed to make Fresh & Easy Shrimp Salad with Lemon, Red Wine Vinegar, & Dill Dressing:
  1. Get 1 cup medium precooked shrimp cocktail type shrimp
  2. Get 1 cup English (Hot House) cucumber that's been seeded and cut into 1/4" cubes
  3. Prepare 1 cup sweet bell pepper (red, orange, or yellow are good) cut into 1/4" pieces
  4. Take 1 cup cherry or grape tomatoes that have been cut in half
  5. Make ready 2/3 cup sweet or red onion cut into 1/4" pieces
  6. Get 3 Tablespoons chopped fresh dill or 1 Tablespoon dry dill
  7. Take 1 Tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  8. Prepare 2 Tablespoons Red Wine Vinegar
  9. Prepare 3/4-1 teaspoon salt
  10. Get 1 teaspoon sugar
  11. Get 1 Tablespoon olive oil

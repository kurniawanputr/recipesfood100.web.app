The ingredients needed to make Fresh & Easy Shrimp Salad with Lemon, Red Wine Vinegar, & Dill Dressing:
  1. Take medium precooked shrimp cocktail type shrimp
  2. Prepare English (Hot House) cucumber that's been seeded and cut into 1/4" cubes
  3. Prepare sweet bell pepper (red, orange, or yellow are good) cut into 1/4" pieces
  4. Prepare cherry or grape tomatoes that have been cut in half
  5. Take sweet or red onion cut into 1/4" pieces
  6. Prepare chopped fresh dill or 1 Tablespoon dry dill
  7. Make ready fresh lemon juice
  8. Prepare Red Wine Vinegar
  9. Take salt
  10. Take sugar
  11. Make ready olive oil

The ingredients needed to make Corned Beef Potatoes:
  1. Get 500 gr potatoes, peeled, diced, steamed until soft, removed from the heat, set aside
  2. Prepare 2 Tbsp butter
  3. Make ready 1/2 onion, minced
  4. Make ready 200 gr corned beef
  5. Prepare 1 medium carrot, small diced
  6. Prepare 100 gr green beans, cut into small pieces
  7. Take 100 gr frozen corn
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt or to taste
  9. Make ready 1/4 tsp white pepper powder or to taste
  10. Get 1/2 tsp nutmeg powder
  11. Prepare 2 stalks green onions, thinly sliced

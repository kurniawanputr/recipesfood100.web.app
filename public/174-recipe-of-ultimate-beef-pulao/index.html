The ingredients needed to make Beef pulao:
  1. Take 1 kg beef
  2. Get 1 kg rice
  3. Make ready 2 onions
  4. Take 1 tbsp garlic paste
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp garam Masala
  6. Make ready to taste Salt
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup dahi
  8. Take 2 tbsp green challi paste
  9. Prepare 1 tsp black pepper sabut
  10. Take 1 tbsp zeera
  11. Make ready 3 green challis sabut
  12. Take 1/2 cup oil

Steps to make Beef pulao:
  1. Beef mein garlic dal kar boil kar lein
  2. Rice ko soaked kar lein
  3. Pot mein oil garam kar ke onion dal kar dark brown hony take fry kry
  4. Jub onion fry ho jaye to is mein zeera dalDein or 2 mint cook kar lein
  5. Ab beef dalDein or sary maslay or dahi dal kar achy say bun lein
  6. Jub masala bun jaye to is mein phly beef ki yakhni daly or agr zarurat ho to thora sa pani dalDein or boil any take pky
  7. (Note) rice ko kasi bartan say map lein or jitny rice ho us say dubal pani daly
  8. Jub pani ko boil a jaye to is mein rice add krDein cover kar ke pky
  9. Jub pani dry ho jaye to rice ke uper green challis rakh kar dum par rakhDein 15 minutes ke lein
  10. Dum say utar ke achy say mix kar lein
  11. Ready to eat enjoy with your family duao mein yad rakhy shukriya

The ingredients needed to make Cajun Shrimp and rice:
  1. Take butter
  2. Get olive oil
  3. Take garlic cloves, minced
  4. Make ready Cajun seasoning
  5. Prepare # shrimp, large, peeled and deveined
  6. Prepare s&p
  7. Get plum tomatoes, chopped
  8. Get scallions, chopped
  9. Make ready asparagus, chopped
  10. Prepare shallot, sliced thin
  11. Make ready parsley, chopped
  12. Take lemon, cut in wedges, for serving
  13. Take white rice
Instructions to make Cajun Shrimp and rice:
  1. Cook the rice according to the package. Heat the butter, oil and garlic in a large skillet over medium-high heat until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add the Cajun seasoning and shrimp and cook, stirring until the shrimp begin to curl, about 1 minute. Season with S&P.
  2. Add the tomatoes, scallions, shallot and asparagus and cook, stirring for 4 minutes so shrimp are opaque. Stir in the parsley and serve over rice. Serve lemon wedges on the side.

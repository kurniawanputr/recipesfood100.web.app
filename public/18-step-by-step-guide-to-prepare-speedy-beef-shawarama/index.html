  4. Make ready 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  6. Prepare 4 garlic gloves
  7. Prepare 1 tablespoon beef shawarma seasonning (if found)
  8. Get Note: In case not found, use the alternative mix:
  9. Get cinnamon powder
  10. Get peppers
  11. Make ready black pepper
  12. Make ready crushed cardamom
  13. Prepare garlic powder
  14. Take dried coriander
  15. Get crushed cumin
  16. Take 1 teaspoon salt
  17. Take Tahini sauce:
  18. Take 1 cup tahini
  19. Get 3/4 cup water
  20. Take 1/4 cup lemon juice
  21. Prepare 1 teaspoon salt
  22. Get 1/2 teaspoon crushed cumin
  23. Get Mix all together untill the paste turn to sauce
  24. Take Note: You can add water if the sauce too thick
  25. Get Biwaz:
  26. Get 1 big sweat onion
  27. Get 4 tablespoon samak1 cup of shopped parsley
  28. Prepare 1 teaspoon olive oil
  29. Prepare For sandwish:
  30. Get french fries
  31. Prepare pickles (long sections)
  32. Prepare Saj bread
Instructions to make Beef_shawarama:
  1. Cut the beef into long and thin chunks.
  2. In a bowl mix the following ingredients: - *crushed garlic - *oil - *apple cedar vinegar - *all the spices mentioned above to form the marinade.
  3. Rub the meat with the marinade, cover and let marinate overnight in the fridge.
  4. In a pan, add the beef marinade, stir on a high heat till well cooked and well dried.
  5. Add the chopped tomato, then stir on a high heat till its done.
  6. Turn off the stove.
  7. Served as sandwishes with addition of pickles and biwaz mix as desired.“

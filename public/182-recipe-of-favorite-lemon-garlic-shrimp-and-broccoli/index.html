The ingredients needed to make Lemon Garlic Shrimp and Broccoli:
  1. Prepare 1 box Farfalle or Angel Hair Pasta
  2. Take 1 Bag Frozen Shrimp
  3. Make ready 1 Bag fresh or Frozen Broccoli
  4. Take Lemon Pepper Seasoning
  5. Make ready Olive Oil
  6. Prepare 1 stick butter
  7. Get 1 1/2 TBSP Minced Garlic
  8. Make ready 1 TBSP Parsley
  9. Make ready 2 TBSP Lemon Juice
  10. Take 1/2 C Heavy Whipping Cream
  11. Take 1/2 Cup Grated Parmesean Cheese
Steps to make Lemon Garlic Shrimp and Broccoli:
  1. Boil Pasta according to package instructions, drain.
  2. De-tail shrimp and pat dry.
  3. Drizzle olive oil on shrimp and toss in lemon pepper seasoning.
  4. Air fry at 360f or bake in oven until they start turning pink/orange and reach proper internal temperature. Mine took around 7 minutes.
  5. Steam your brocolli. I did mine in the microwave in a bowl with a 1/4c water for 4 minutes.
  6. Melt butter in a sauce pan and add minced garlic and Parsley. Simmer for 5 minutes.
  7. Stir lemon juice and heavy whipping cream into the sauce and simmer for 2 more minutes.
  8. Pour sauce and parmesean into prepared noodles. Add the broccoli and shrimp and stir to combine.
  9. Serve immediately and enjoy with another dash of lemon and parmesean.

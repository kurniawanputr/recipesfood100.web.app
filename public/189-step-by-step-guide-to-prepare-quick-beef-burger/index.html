Steps to make Beef burger:
  1. Mix your minced meat,diced onions,and spices together add the egg
  2. Make balls with mixture then flatten then into 2 thick circles. Let it stick together for 2 hrs
  3. Fry the minced meat for 5 mins while turning on medium heat.
  4. Cut the buns into 2 halves and fry them slightly
  5. Layer your burger with onions lettuce and tomatoes,can add tomato sauce or mayo, add the cooked but moist meat

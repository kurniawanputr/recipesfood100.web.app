The ingredients needed to make Beef Rotini (Slow Cooker):
  1. Make ready 2 lbs ground beef
  2. Take 24 oz marinara
  3. Make ready 1 tsp dried Italian seasoning
  4. Take 1/2 cup chopped onion, red and green bell peppers, frozen
  5. Make ready 2 tsp garlic, minced
  6. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp black pepper
  8. Get 3 cup water
  9. Make ready 1 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  10. Prepare 2 cup rotini noodles, uncooked
  11. Get 1/2 cup heavy whipping cream
  12. Take 1 1/2 cups Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  13. Prepare 3/4 cup Parmesan cheese, shredded
Instructions to make Beef Rotini (Slow Cooker):
  1. Break up ground beef and put into pot.
  2. Add marinara sauce, tomatoes, Italian seasoning, frozen vegetables, minced garlic, salt, black pepper, water & olive oil to pot.
  3. Stir until combined.
  4. Close & lock the lid.
  5. Press the steam release valve to open.
  6. Press SLOW COOK—Normal; Timer: 5 hours
  7. Press CANCEL (Keep Warm: On)
  8. Stir in rotini pasta and heavy whipping cream.
  9. Top with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese
  10. Close & lock the lid.
  11. Press the steam release valve to open.
  12. Press SLOW COOK—Normal; Timer: 1 hour
  13. Press CANCEL (Keep Warm: On)
  14. Stir until well combined.
  15. Serve warm.

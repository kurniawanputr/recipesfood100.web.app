The ingredients needed to make Beef Stew:
  1. Make ready Half kg beef brisket
  2. Make ready Half red and half green bell pepper
  3. Take Onion
  4. Make ready Peppercorns
  5. Prepare Tomato sauce
  6. Get 1 tsp Oyster sauce
Instructions to make Beef Stew:
  1. Boil beef for an hour add peppercorns.Don't throw the stock, can use it later. When beef is tender. Ready the wok heat it and add oil. Saute onion and beef. Then pour oyster sauce and tomato sauce.Pour some beef stock. Simmer it
  2. Next add bell pepper cook it half done.

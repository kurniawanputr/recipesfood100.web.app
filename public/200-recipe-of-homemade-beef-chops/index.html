The ingredients needed to make Beef Chops:
  1. Prepare 1 kg beef ke Chops
  2. Take 3 tblspoon dahi
  3. Make ready 2 tblspoon lehsan adrak ka paste
  4. Make ready 2 tblspoon hari mirchon ka paste
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon garam masala powder
  6. Take 1 teaspoon haldi powder
  7. Take 1 tblspoon lal mirch powder
  8. Take As required salt
  9. Prepare 1 teaspoon chat masala
  10. Make ready 1 teaspoon bhoona hua kuta hua zeera
  11. Take 2 eggs
  12. Make ready Oil for frying
Steps to make Beef Chops:
  1. Chops ko wash kar ke dry kar lain
  2. Phir aik bowl mein dahi, lehsan adrak ka paste,green chilli ka paste,garam masala powder,haldi powder,lal mirch powder, chat masala,salt,zeera, dal kar mix karen
  3. Phir is mein 2 tblspoon oil dalen aur achi tarah mix kar ke chops mein lagalain masala aur maranid kar lain
  4. Over night bhi rakh sakte hain aur 2 hour bhi
  5. Phir patili mein ye maranid ki hui chops dalenaur gla lain masala dry ho jaye ga aur chipak jaye ga
  6. Phir dish mein nikal kar thanda kar lain
  7. Aik bowl main eggs beat karen chootki salt dal kar
  8. Aur karahi mein oil dalen garam karein phir chops ko andey main deep kar kar ke oil main dalen

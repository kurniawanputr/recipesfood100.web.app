The ingredients needed to make Beef Stew:

  2. Prepare 2 large onion
  3. Get 3-4 large tomato
  4. Make ready 3 green chilli chopped
  5. Take 4 green chilli for dum
  6. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder and flakes

  8. Get 1/4 teaspoon coriander powder
  9. Get 3 green cardamom
  10. Prepare 1 large black cardamom
  11. Take 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  12. Prepare 1 teaspoon cloves
  13. Prepare 1-2 bay leaves
  14. Get 1 medium stick cinnamon
  15. Get 1 tablespoon cumin seeds
  16. Prepare 1 1/2 tablespoons button red chilli
  17. Prepare 1 (1 tablespoon) ginger garlic paste
  18. Make ready 2 tablespoons curd
  19. Take 1 tablespoon dry fenugreek leaves
  20. Take 3 tablespoons julienne cut ginger
  21. Take 1/2 glass water
  22. Take to taste Salt
  23. Get 1/3 cup oil
Instructions to make Beef Stew:
  1. Sary ingredients phly samny rakh lyn is se apko khana bnany me bht asani rehti hy. Aik pot me oil dalyn usay garam karyn aur phr onion dal ke fry karyn. Brown nhn karni hai bssss translucent karna hai.
  2. Jab onion soft hojaye to us me sara garam masala add kardyn aur 1 minute tak fry karyn medium flame ginger garlic paste dal ke fry karyn par us ke bad meat add kardyn aur halka sa fry karyn.
  3. Ab tamater aur hari mirchyn chopped kar ke dalyn aur tamatar ke 4 pieces karlyn. Ya half kar ke bhi add kar skty hyn.aur sary spices bhi add kardyn.

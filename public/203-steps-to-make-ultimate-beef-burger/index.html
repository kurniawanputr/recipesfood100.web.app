The ingredients needed to make Beef burger:
  1. Prepare 1 kg mince meat
  2. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  3. Get 1 tbsp worster sauce
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp mustard paste
  5. Make ready 3 tbsp bread crumb
  6. Prepare 1 small onion
  7. Take 1 tbsp black pepper
  8. Prepare 1 tsp Oregano leaves
  9. Get 1 tbsp hot sauce

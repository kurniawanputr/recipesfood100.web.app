The ingredients needed to make One Sheet Sausage and Shrimp:
  1. Make ready 16 Oz Raw Large Shrimp, Peeled, Tail Off
  2. Get 14 Oz Smoked Sausage, Sliced
  3. Make ready 1.5 Petite Red Potatoes
  4. Prepare 3 Corn Ears (cut into thirds)
  5. Make ready 1 Stick Butter (melted)
  6. Take 1 Tbsp Old Bay Seasoning
  7. Get 1 Tbsp Minced Garlic
  8. Get 1 Tbsp Parsley

Instructions to make One Sheet Sausage and Shrimp:
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. In a large pot, bring water to boil and cook potatoes 10-15 minutes. Add corn and boil for 5 additional minutes.
  3. Melt butter and mix with garlic, old bay, and parsley.
  4. Combine potatoes, corn, shrimp, and sausage in large mixing bowl and toss with butter mixture.
  5. Pour mixture onto a large baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes or until shrimp is opaque.
  6. Serve with lemon wedges and enjoy!

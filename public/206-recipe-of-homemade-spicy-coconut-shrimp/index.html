The ingredients needed to make Spicy Coconut Shrimp:
  1. Take 20 Cleaned/ Unclean Shrimps
  2. Take 1 Cup dry Coconut Flakes/ or Freshly Grated Coconut
  3. Take 2 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  4. Take 1 tsp White Pepper
  5. Take 2 tsp Complete Seasoning
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup Coconut Milk
  7. Prepare 2 oz Butter
  8. Take 1 cup Water
Steps to make Spicy Coconut Shrimp:
  1. If Shrimps are not cleaned please do so and remember to remove the intestines.
  2. Add all the dry ingredients to your pan and let marinate for at lease 5 minutes.
  3. Add Butter, Milk and Water to the pan.
  4. Cover with foil and place in oven @200°F. (This could also be done on the stove top for faster cooking.)
  5. Cook for 10 minutes.
  6. After the 10 minutes taste the pot to see if the flavors are in your liking. (If you want more spice you could add some hot sauce to the mixture aswell)
  7. Recover the pan and let simmer until 1/2 the liquids in the pan has dissolved.
  8. Serve with any side you desire.

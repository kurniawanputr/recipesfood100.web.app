The ingredients needed to make Beef chops:
  1. Prepare 1 kg Chops
  2. Make ready 3 Tomatoes
  3. Take 2 onion
  4. Prepare 2 Green chili
  5. Prepare 1 bowl Yogurt (medium)
  6. Take 1 tablespoon Garlic
  7. Make ready 1 tablespoon Ginger
  8. Get 1 teaspoon Crushed coriander
  9. Make ready 1 teaspoon Red chili flakes
  10. Prepare 1 teaspoon Turmeric
  11. Prepare Salt as per taste
  12. Make ready 1 teaspoon Crushed cumin
  13. Make ready 1 teaspoon Crushed black pepper
  14. Make ready 1 teaspoon Hot spice
  15. Take 1 cup Oil
  16. Get 1 cup Water

The ingredients needed to make Onion Beef:
  1. Get strips Beef,
  2. Get To taste Black pepper
  3. Make ready To taste Salt
  4. Prepare Half onion, strips
Steps to make Onion Beef:
  1. Marinate beef with salt. Heat wok then add cooking oil.
  2. Make sure cooking oil is hot before adding beef. Quickly stir fry beef until color changes.
  3. Then remove. Then stir fry onion until translucent then add beef.
  4. Sprinkle black pepper and salt to taste. Mix well then remove.

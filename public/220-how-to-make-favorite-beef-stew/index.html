The ingredients needed to make Beef stew:
  1. Make ready 600 g McCain Stew mix
  2. Take 500 g beef cubes
  3. Take 1 Onion, chopped
  4. Prepare 30 ml olive oil
  5. Take 1 teaspoon coriander
  6. Take 1 packet (10 g) tomato paste
  7. Get 1 can tomato and onion
  8. Get 2 beef stock cubes
  9. Make ready 120 ml hot water
  10. Prepare 240 ml hot water

Steps to make Beef stew:
  1. Heat the oil and fry beef cubes until tender and brown.
  2. Stir in onions and coriander and fry for 10 minutes.
  3. Dissolve stock cubes in 120ml water.
  4. Add to the beef mixture, tomato and onions, stock and bring to the boil.
  5. Add stew mix and 240ml water and allow to simmer until meat is tender and veggies cooked. Stir occasionally.
  6. Serve and enjoy.

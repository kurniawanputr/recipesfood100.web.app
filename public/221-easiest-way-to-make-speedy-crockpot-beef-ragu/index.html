The ingredients needed to make Crockpot Beef Ragu:
  1. Prepare 1 large pack ground beef
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup red wine
  3. Take 1 onion, chopped
  4. Prepare 1 green pepper, chopped
  5. Prepare 10 garlic cloves, minced
  6. Prepare 1 can (28 ounce) crushed tomatoes
  7. Take 1 can (14.5 ounce) diced tomatoes
  8. Get 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  9. Prepare 1 tablespoon oregano
  10. Take 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  11. Take 1/2 teaspoon basil leaves
  12. Prepare 1/2 cup heavy cream
  13. Take 1 box (16 ounce) pasta
  14. Get Salt for boiling water
  15. Take Parmesan cheese for topping

The ingredients needed to make Shrimp jellof rice:
  1. Get 1 kilo Shrimps
  2. Make ready 4 cups Long grain rice
  3. Prepare 1 canned Carrots
  4. Make ready 1 canned Peas
  5. Take 1 Chopped onions
  6. Get 1/3 cup Soy sauce
  7. Take to taste Salt and seasoning
  8. Make ready 1 table spoon Ginger and garlic paste
  9. Take 1/2 cup Vegitable oil
  10. Prepare 2 cups Water
  11. Take 1/2 cup oyster sauce
  12. Get 1/2 cup sesame oil
  13. Get 1 cup neatly chopped spring onions

Steps to make Shrimp jellof rice:
  1. Add oil to the pan
  2. Add the shrimps and stir fry till the water dries up
  3. Add the onions and ginger garlic paste
  4. Add carrots and peas
  5. Add the soy sauce, plus the salt and seasoning
  6. Mix thoroughly and stir fry for 3 minutes
  7. Add water and bring to a boil
  8. Add the pre washed and drained rice then add to the pot
  9. Lower the heat and cover air tight
  10. Cook for 20 minutes
  11. Add oyster sauce, sesame oil, spring onions and give it a toss
  12. Serve

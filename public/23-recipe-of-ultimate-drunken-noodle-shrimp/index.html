The ingredients needed to make Drunken noodle (shrimp):
  1. Take 1 pack flake rice noodle
  2. Prepare 7-10 pcs shrimp
  3. Prepare Half bell peper
  4. Get Little bit of white onion
  5. Take Little bit of green onion
  6. Get 1 tbps soy sauce
  7. Get 1 tbps oyster sauce
  8. Make ready 1 tbps mushroom sauce
  9. Take 1-2 eggs

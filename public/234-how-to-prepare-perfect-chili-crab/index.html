The ingredients needed to make Chili Crab:
  1. Make ready 500 gm Mud Crab
  2. Take 1 pc Lemongrass
  3. Get 300 ml Water
  4. Prepare 1 egg
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp Salt
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp sugar
  7. Get 5 tbsp Tomato Sauce
  8. Take 4 tbsp Chili Sauce
  9. Prepare 8 pcs Chopped Chili
  10. Get 1/2 tsp Vinegar
  11. Take 4 tbsp Cooking Oil
Instructions to make Chili Crab:
  1. Heat up the wok and placed all the ingredients into the wok except the crab.
  2. Once it's boiled then put in the crab and covered for 10 to 15 minutes
  3. Put in an egg and stirring and ready to be served

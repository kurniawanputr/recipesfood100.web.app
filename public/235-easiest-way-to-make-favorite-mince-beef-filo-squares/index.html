The ingredients needed to make Mince beef filo squares:
  1. Prepare For Beef filling
  2. Take 500 gms lean beef mince
  3. Prepare 1 tbls garlic paste
  4. Make ready 1 habanero chilli paste
  5. Get to taste Salt
  6. Make ready 1/4 cup finely chopped cilantro
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup finely chopped red onions
  8. Get 1 tbls lemon/lime juice
  9. Take 1 large egg
  10. Take 1 tbls vegetable oil
  11. Make ready 2 tbls vegetable ghee
  12. Get 6 sheets filo pastry or 125 gms of filo pastry

Steps to make Mince beef filo squares:
  1. To prepare filling, heat vegetable oil to a heavy bottom pot over medium-high heat. Sauté garlic until golden.
  2. Add mince beef and habanero chilli paste to the pot and stir to combine and cook over medium heat until mince is cooked.
  3. Turn heat off and add lemon juice, salt, cilantro and onions and stir to combine. Set aside till mince filling has cooled down, add one beaten egg and stir to combine
  4. Begin assembling your dish: in an oven proof dish, brush ghee at the bottom of the dish and layer half of the filo pastry (between each layer, brush with ghee). Add mince beef mixture and repeat with layering the rest of the filo pastry. Before baking, brush the rest of the ghee over the top of the pastry
  5. In a preheated oven (180 degrees Celsius). Bake the dish for 45-50 minutes, or until filo pastry turns golden brown.
  6. Slice the dish and serve warm with lemon/lime wedges

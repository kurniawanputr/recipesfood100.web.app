The ingredients needed to make Quick Imitation Crabmeat Sandwiches:
  1. Take 2 packages Imitation CrabMeat(I used 2 - 2 lb. packages)
  2. Prepare 3/4 cup mayonnaise
  3. Make ready 1/2 tablespoon garlic powder
  4. Make ready 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  5. Take 1 tablespoon milk OR half and half
  6. Take Sprinkle Black Pepper
  7. Make ready Sprinkle parsley flakes
Instructions to make Quick Imitation Crabmeat Sandwiches:
  1. Place imitation crab meat into a large bowl, breaking up any large pieces……
  2. Add to bowl : mayonnaise, garlic powder, cayenne pepper, black pepper, parsley flakes and milk or half and half, mix all very well…..
  3. After mixing cover and place bowl into refrigerator to chill for about 2 hours…….Stir again before serving…..

The ingredients needed to make Beefy Spaghetti & Shaved Mushrooms:
  1. Take 1 lb ground beef
  2. Prepare 1 lb spaghetti
  3. Take 3-4 Tbsp flour
  4. Take 1/4 cup olive
  5. Get 1/2 tea black pepper
  6. Make ready Kosher salt
  7. Get hot water
  8. Take 1 lb mushrooms of choice
  9. Get yellow onion
  10. Make ready red -yellow bell peppers
  11. Make ready 3 garlic cloves(chopped)
Steps to make Beefy Spaghetti & Shaved Mushrooms:
  1. Cook ground beef in cast iron skillet or, any non stick add peppers, garlic and, onions drain and set aside.
  2. In the same skillet add olive oil heat then, add then using a wire whisk stir an make sure there are no lumps, when flour turns dark brown add, water till u get the thick or, thinnest you want it.
  3. Once you've done that add meat mixture to gravy, turn heat down and, let simmer; Add water to med pot and, add salt boil then, add spaghetti when spaghetti is a I aldente drain and add to meat and, gravy mixture then salt to taste, shave mushrooms and, enjoy

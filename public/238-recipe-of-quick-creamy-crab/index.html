The ingredients needed to make Creamy Crab:
  1. Prepare 1 kg Crab
  2. Take 1 Onion Leaks
  3. Make ready 1 Small White Onion
  4. Take 3 Green chili sliced
  5. Get 3 Cloves Garlic
  6. Make ready 1/4 Butter
  7. Prepare 3 T Vegetable oil
  8. Make ready 1 Coconut Cream
  9. Prepare 1 tps Pepper
  10. Prepare 4 T salt
Instructions to make Creamy Crab:
  1. Make sure the crab are clean then cut the crab into two pieces
  2. Onion leaks slice in thin
  3. Cut the onion at middle then slice again till the onion run out
  4. Choped green chili
  5. Make sure the garlic are mince
  6. Heat pan when the is heat add oil
  7. Add butter sauté onion until it's translucent, add garlic sauté until golden brown
  8. Add green chili, onion leaks, add the water cream coconut boil till it drain
  9. The add pure cream coconut boil for 10 -15 minutes.

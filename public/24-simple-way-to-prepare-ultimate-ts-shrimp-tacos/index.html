The ingredients needed to make T’s Shrimp Tacos:
  1. Take For Shrimp
  2. Take 1 bag extra large peeled/deveined shrimp
  3. Make ready Your favorite Cajun seasoning
  4. Prepare Garlic Powder
  5. Make ready Salt
  6. Make ready Pepper
  7. Take Old bay
  8. Make ready 2 tablespoons olive oil
  9. Get For Cole Slaw
  10. Prepare Tricolor bag of cole slaw
  11. Get 1 tablespoon Honey
  12. Make ready 1-2 tablespoons Mayonnaise
  13. Prepare The juice of 1 lime
  14. Make ready 1 Teaspoon rice vinegar
  15. Take to taste Salt and pepper
  16. Prepare For Shell
  17. Get Yellow corn tortillas
  18. Take Toppings
  19. Make ready Fresh Cilantro
  20. Take Cojita Cheese
  21. Get Mexican blend shredded cheese
  22. Prepare slices Avocado
  23. Take Fresh lime
  24. Make ready Aji Amarillo Sauce (ingredients to follow)
  25. Prepare 2 jalapeños (leave some seeds, but not all)
  26. Make ready 1 cup cilantro
  27. Prepare 1 tablespoon aji Amarillo paste
  28. Make ready 2 tablespoons cotija cheese
  29. Take 1 clove garlic
  30. Make ready 1 teaspoon white vinegar
  31. Take 3 teaspoons fresh lime juice
  32. Get 1/2 cup mayo
  33. Take Salt/pepper
Steps to make T’s Shrimp Tacos:
  1. Place shrimp in a pan with the olive oil. Season with Cajun, old bay, garlic, salt and pepper. Sear until brown on both sides. Set aside.
  2. Open bag of cole slaw into a mixing bowl. Add mayo, honey, lime juice, rice vinegar, salt and pepper. Mix well. Set aside
  3. For Aji Amarillo - don’t skip this! This is the perfect sauce for these! Place all ingredients into a food processor and pulse until mixed.
  4. Toast tortillas in a hot pan until they start to brown on both sides
  5. Assemble with cole slaw, avacado, cotija, shredded Mexican cheese, aji Amarillo, fresh squeeze of lime, and a sprig of fresh cilantro!

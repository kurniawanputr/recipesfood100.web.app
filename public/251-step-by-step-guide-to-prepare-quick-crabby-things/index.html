The ingredients needed to make Crabby Things:
  1. Get 5 oz Kraft Old English cheese
  2. Get 6 oz can white crab meat (drained)
  3. Get 1 stick butter
  4. Get 1 package English muffins
  5. Take Old bay seasoning
Steps to make Crabby Things:
  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Melt butter in small saucepan. Add cheese and crab meat and allow to simmer on a low heat for 5 minutes while stirring.
  3. Arrange English muffins onto a large cookie sheet.
  4. Spread cheese mixture onto English muffins and top with old bay seasoning.
  5. Bake for approximately 13-15 minutes or until edges of English muffins are a golden brown.
  6. Cut English muffins into quarters and serve as a hot appetizer.

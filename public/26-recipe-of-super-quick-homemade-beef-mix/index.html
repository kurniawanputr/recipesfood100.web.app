The ingredients needed to make Beef Mix:
  1. Prepare Beef
  2. Take 1 leeks
  3. Take Half red bell pepper
  4. Take Black pepper
  5. Make ready Cooking wine
  6. Take Just a little salt
Steps to make Beef Mix:
  1. Marinate beef with black pepper and salt. Fry it first then set aside.
  2. Stir fry leeks and add a little water. Simmer until half done then add bell pepper. When done add beef and mix. Simmer just a minute. Serve

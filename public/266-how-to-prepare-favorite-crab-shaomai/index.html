The ingredients needed to make Crab Shaomai:
  1. Make ready 300 g minced pork
  2. Take 20 cm spring onion
  3. Prepare 3 tablespoon tapioca starch (any starch ok)
  4. Take 1/2 tablespoon oyster sauce
  5. Prepare sheet shaomai
  6. Prepare 1 small tin crab
Instructions to make Crab Shaomai:
  1. Chop spring onion. This is easy way to chop spring onion.
  2. Mix all ingredients with hand.
  3. Make a circle with your thumb and forefinger and place the shaomai sheet on it. Put meat mixture on it and make shapes.
  4. Steam for 5 minutes in pressure pan or 15 minutes in steam pan.

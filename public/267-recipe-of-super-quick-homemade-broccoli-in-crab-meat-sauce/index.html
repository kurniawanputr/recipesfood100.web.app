The ingredients needed to make Broccoli in crab meat sauce:
  1. Make ready 1 head crab
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp corn flour
  3. Take 1 head broccoli
  4. Prepare 2 clove garlic
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup chicken stock
  6. Get 1 tsp soya sauce
  7. Prepare 1 dash salt

Steps to make Broccoli in crab meat sauce:
  1. boil half pot of water
  2. add broccoli , blanched
  3. wash n cut the crab into half
  4. when crab is cooked remove the meat n put in bowl
  5. fry the garlic, add chicken stock
  6. mix corn flour with a bit of water
  7. add corn flour mix to boiling stock
  8. add crab meat to stock
  9. stir n add seasoning then pour on broccoli

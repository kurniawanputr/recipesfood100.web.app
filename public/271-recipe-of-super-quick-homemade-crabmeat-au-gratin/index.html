The ingredients needed to make Crabmeat Au Gratin:
  1. Take 1/2 cup butter
  2. Make ready 1 stalk celery chopped
  3. Prepare 1 cup chopped onion
  4. Take 1/2 cup flour
  5. Prepare 1 can (13 oz) evaporated milk
  6. Take 2 egg yolks
  7. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp red pepper
  9. Prepare 1/4 tsp black pepper
  10. Take 1 lb lump crabmeat
  11. Prepare 1/2 lb grated cheddar cheese
Instructions to make Crabmeat Au Gratin:
  1. Preheat oven 375°F.
  2. Saute onions & celery in butter til wilted.
  3. Blend in flour.
  4. Pour in milk gradually stirring constantly.
  5. Add egg yolks and seasonings and cook for 5 minutes.
  6. Add crabmeat & cheese and blend well.
  7. Put in greased casserole dish and bake for 10 to 15 minutes.

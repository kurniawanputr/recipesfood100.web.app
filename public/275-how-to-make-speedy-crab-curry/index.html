The ingredients needed to make Crab curry:
  1. Prepare 500 gms Crab
  2. Make ready 1 Tomato
  3. Make ready 2 cups Coconut milk
  4. Make ready 2 tablespoons Gingelly oil
  5. Make ready 1 sprig Curry leaves
  6. Take For grinding:
  7. Take 1 Onion
  8. Prepare 1 tablespoon Ginger garlic paste
  9. Get 1 teaspoon Red chilli powder
  10. Prepare 1 teaspoon Coriander powder
  11. Take 1/2 teaspoon Cumin and fennel powder
  12. Take 1 teaspoon Curry masala powder

Steps to make Crab curry:
  1. Grind all the ingredients as given under the grinding section
  2. Add oil in the pan,gingelly oil is recommended. Once the oil got heated add curry leaves and the grinded paste
  3. Saute the paste until the raw smell gone and add some water, add chopped tomato and salt as required and fry the tomato until it becomes soft and mushy. Check the taste for salt and spices at this point and correct it.
  4. Once the tomatoes are well cooked add crabs into the gravy and cover the lid for 7 to 10 mins approximately.
  5. When the crabs are half cooked, add 2 cups of coconut milk and let crabs cook completely for 10 to 12 mins in medium flame approximately. Nice crab curry is ready. It can be served with rice, idli and dosa. Note: For thick curry add only one cup of coconut milk

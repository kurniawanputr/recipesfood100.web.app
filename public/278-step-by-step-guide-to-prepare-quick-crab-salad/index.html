The ingredients needed to make Crab Salad:
  1. Prepare 1 lb imitation crab meat
  2. Get 1 celery stalks, chopped
  3. Get 1/4 cup onion, minced
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp ranch seasoning
  5. Make ready 2 tsp Old Bay
  6. Take Juice from half a lemon
  7. Take 2 tbsp mayo (more if you like it creamier)
Steps to make Crab Salad:
  1. Chop up your crab
  2. Mix everything together. Refrigerate atleast an hour (I prefer 3 hours)
  3. Eat with crackers, crostinis, bread.. whatever floats your crab boat.

The ingredients needed to make Salted Egg Crab:
  1. Take 2 crabs
  2. Make ready 1 spring onion (slice)
  3. Prepare 1 ginger (slice)
  4. Get SAUCE:
  5. Get 4 salted egg yolk (steamed and mashed)
  6. Take 1 tbsp butter
  7. Take 1 tbsp oil
  8. Take 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  9. Get 5 curry leaves
  10. Take 2 eye-bird chilli (optional)
  11. Take 1 can evaporated milk (390 g)
  12. Get Salt
  13. Prepare Sugar

Instructions to make Salted Egg Crab:
  1. Steam the crab with ginger and spring onion 12 mins. Put aside.
  2. Heat oil and butter. Add garlic, curry leaves and chilli until fragrant.
  3. Add evaporated milk, salt, pepper, mashed egg yolk. Stir and cook until the sauce gets thickened.
  4. Add the crab and toss well to coat. Ready to serve.

The ingredients needed to make Crab Curry:
  1. Get 4 Crabs pieces
  2. Make ready 1 spoon Cumin and coriander powder
  3. Take 1 Onion
  4. Prepare 2 Tomato
  5. Get as needed Ginger slice
  6. Make ready 4-5 Garlic cloves
  7. Take 1 spoon Garam masala powder
  8. Prepare pinch Turmeric powder
  9. Take to taste Salt and sugar

Steps to make Crab Curry:
  1. Boil water. Soak crabs in it after washing thoroughly.
  2. Chop tomato, onion. Boil oil in pan and fry onions.
  3. Blend tomato, garlic cloves, ginger slice. Transfer the smooth paste into oil when the onion turns brown.
  4. Add cumin coriander powder, turmeric powder and salt and sugar.
  5. Cook everything till the raw smell goes away.
  6. Add crabs. Cover for a while.
  7. Add garam masala powder.

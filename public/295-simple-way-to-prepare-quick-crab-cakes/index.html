The ingredients needed to make Crab Cakes:
  1. Prepare 1 envelope blue star lump crab meat
  2. Make ready 3 stalk green onion
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp panko bread crumbs
  4. Get 2 tbsp italian bread crumbs
  5. Prepare dash dried dill
  6. Take pinch salt & pepper
  7. Take pinch cayenne pepper
  8. Take 1 large egg
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp regular mayo
  10. Get yellow mustard
  11. Prepare lemon juice
Steps to make Crab Cakes:
  1. Mix everything but the crab together. Mix well. Taste to see if additional seasoning is needed.
  2. Add the crab last. Mix well but try not to break up the crab.
  3. Form 4 crab patty's. It will be more wet than dry.
  4. Put in a preheated skillet. Bake for about 10 minutes than flip.
  5. Garnish with lemon aioli & additional green onions.
  6. Lemon aioli: mayo, lemon juice & EVOO. Equal parts mayo & EVOO. Add lemon juice to taste & season with salt & pepper.

The ingredients needed to make “Beefy” Beefless Swiss Chard Recipe:
  1. Prepare 2 tbsp olive oil
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp butter
  3. Get 1 bunch Swiss chard
  4. Take 2 cloves garlic, minced
  5. Prepare 2 shallots or 1/4th cup onions
  6. Get 1/2 tsp beef stock or bouillon powder
  7. Prepare to taste Salt

Steps to make “Beefy” Beefless Swiss Chard Recipe:
  1. Cut 1/2 inch pieces of chard, separating stems from the leaves
  2. Toss olive oil, onions and stem pieces and cook for 5 minutes over medium heat.
  3. Add butter and garlic and sauté for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Throw in leaves until and cook covered for a few minutes until leaves have wilted down.
  5. Add beef powder and mix well. Taste and add more beef powder or salt if desired.
  6. Enjoy!

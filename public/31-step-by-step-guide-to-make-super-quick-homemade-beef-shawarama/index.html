Steps to make Beef_shawarama:
  1. Cut the beef into long and thin chunks.
  2. In a bowl mix the following ingredients: - *crushed garlic - *oil - *apple cedar vinegar - *all the spices mentioned above to form the marinade.
  3. Rub the meat with the marinade, cover and let marinate overnight in the fridge.
  4. In a pan, add the beef marinade, stir on a high heat till well cooked and well dried.
  5. Add the chopped tomato, then stir on a high heat till its done.
  6. Turn off the stove.
  7. Served as sandwishes with addition of pickles and biwaz mix as desired.“

The ingredients needed to make CrabCakes:
  1. Take 2 Egg
  2. Prepare 3 tbsp mayonnaise
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp mustard
  4. Prepare 1 pinch chopped parsley
  5. Get 2 can crab meat
  6. Make ready 2 pinch oregano
  7. Make ready 1 pinch chopped mint leaves
  8. Make ready 1 quart bread crumbs

Steps to make CrabCakes:
  1. Combine all ingredients together in a big bowl and mix but not to much
  2. Mold into 2 ounce round circles
  3. Bread one more time both sides
  4. Sear on both sides for about 1,minute and put into the oven for 5 minutes on 350°
  5. serve to your liking and enjoy

The ingredients needed to make Crab Dip:
  1. Take 1 envelope Dry Ranch Dip mix
  2. Make ready 16 oz mayonnaise (not miracle whip)
  3. Get 8 oz cream cheese (softened)
  4. Prepare 16 oz sour cream
  5. Prepare 16 oz imitation flake style crab meat
  6. Take 2 tbsp lemon juice

Steps to make Crab Dip:
  1. Tear the crab meat into the size pieces you like and put aside.
  2. Mix the rest of the ingredients with a mixer on medium till all the chunks are gone.
  3. Stir in the crab meat. Refrigerate until cold and set up.
  4. Serve with crackers tortilla chips or whatever you like. We like the bite sized Tostitos brand tortilla chips the best.

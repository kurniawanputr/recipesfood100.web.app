The ingredients needed to make Crab fry:
  1. Take 200 grams crab
  2. Get 2 tablespoons rice flour
  3. Get 1 teaspoon haldi
  4. Take 1 teaspoon jeera powder
  5. Take 1 teaspoon Dhaniya powder
  6. Make ready 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  7. Get 1 chop green chilli
  8. Get 1/2 teaspoon Garam Masala
  9. Prepare as per taste Salt
  10. Get 2 tablespoons crushed curry leaves
  11. Prepare 7 tablespoons oil

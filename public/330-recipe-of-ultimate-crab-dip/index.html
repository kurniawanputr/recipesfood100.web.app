The ingredients needed to make Crab Dip:
  1. Prepare Dip
  2. Take 16 oz cream cheese
  3. Take 1 1/2 tbsp Dill
  4. Make ready 1 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  5. Take 12 oz canned crab meat
  6. Make ready Topping
  7. Take 2 cup cheddar cheese (grated)
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup green onion (chopped)
  9. Take 2 medium tomatoes (seeds removed)

The ingredients needed to make Fried Beef:
  1. Get 7 slices beef
  2. Prepare Salt
  3. Take Black pepper
  4. Take Olive oil
Steps to make Fried Beef:
  1. Rub beef with a little salt and black pepper
  2. Heat pan and pour a little olive oil. Make sure that the pan is hot before fry beef
  3. Fry beef 3 mins in each side then remove

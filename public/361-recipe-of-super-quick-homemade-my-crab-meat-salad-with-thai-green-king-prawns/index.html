The ingredients needed to make My Crab meat salad with Thai Green king Prawns 😙:
  1. Get 50 g Crab meat Cooked
  2. Make ready Handful mixed lettuce
  3. Make ready 4 cherry Tomatoes
  4. Take 2 tbls chopped yellow sweet pepper
  5. Take 1 tsp lemon juice
  6. Take 2 tbls Seafood tangy sauce
Steps to make My Crab meat salad with Thai Green king Prawns 😙:
  1. Add the Crab meat to a mixing bowl add lettuce and tomatoes
  2. Next add sweet pepper
  3. Then the seafood sauce
  4. Mix in the lemon juice
  5. Then set out in a serving bowl
  6. Add to a serving plate with Thai Green king Prawns in Breadcrumbs.
  7. Enjoy it.

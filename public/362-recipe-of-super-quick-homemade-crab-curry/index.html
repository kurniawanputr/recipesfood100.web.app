The ingredients needed to make Crab curry:
  1. Prepare 1 kg crab
  2. Take 1-2 Onion medium size chopped
  3. Get 2 Tomatoes medium size chopped
  4. Take 2 tsp turmeric powder
  5. Prepare 2 tsp chilli powder
  6. Prepare 2 green chilli
  7. Get 3-4 tsp meat masala
  8. Make ready 2 tsp cumin powder
  9. Get 2 tsp coriander powder
  10. Take 1 bay leaf
  11. Prepare 2 clove
  12. Prepare 2 cardamom
  13. Get to taste salt
  14. Take 3 tsp ginger garlic paste
  15. Prepare as needed Coriander leaves
  16. Take as needed mustard oil

Instructions to make Crab curry:
  1. Heat oil in a kadhai and add cardamom, bay leaf, clove. add onion and fry until golden brown. add ginger garlic paste and tomato fry until becomes soft.
  2. Then add chilli powder, green chillies, turmeric powder, salt to taste, cumin powder, coriander powder & fry them 5 mins.
  3. Now add in cleaned crab and toss well in the masala.
  4. Add a little heated water & Cover and cook for 20 mins..
  5. Finally, Add meat masala and turn off the gas. Now add coriander leaves and serve with rice.

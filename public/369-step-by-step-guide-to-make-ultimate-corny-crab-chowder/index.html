The ingredients needed to make Corny Crab Chowder:
  1. Get 8 oz fresh crab meat or refrigerated canned crab meat
  2. Make ready 2 T olive oil
  3. Make ready 5-6 russet potatoes, peeled and chopped (about 13 oz)
  4. Take 1 small onion, chopped
  5. Prepare 48 oz low-sodium chicken broth
  6. Get 1-1/2 c frozen corn
  7. Prepare 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
  8. Make ready 1 T dried basil
  9. Get 1/2 c half and half
  10. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  11. Prepare 1/4 tsp pepper
  12. Take chopped fresh parsley or dried oregano to garnish

Steps to make Corny Crab Chowder:
  1. In large pot, heat oil. Add potatoes and onions. Cook until onion is tender. Add broth and simmer 20 min.
  2. Add remaining ingredients and cook on low 5 min. Serve with garnish if using.

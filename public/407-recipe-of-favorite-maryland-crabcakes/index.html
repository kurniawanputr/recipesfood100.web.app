The ingredients needed to make Maryland Crabcakes:
  1. Make ready 1 lbs. Crabmeat (Backfin / Lump, picked over for shell
  2. Prepare 1/3 Cup Mayonnaise
  3. Make ready 1 small dice Red bell pepper
  4. Prepare 1 small dice Red onion
  5. Get 2 tsp. Old Bay Seasoning or Philips Blackened Seasoning
  6. Get 1 tsp. Dry Mustard
  7. Take 1 Egg
  8. Make ready 2 tsp. Worcestershire Sauce
  9. Prepare 2 tsp Lemon juice
  10. Make ready 1/4 Cup Wondra flour

The ingredients needed to make Winter Shrimp Crab Boil:
  1. Take 24 oz shrimp at least
  2. Prepare 2 lemons…quartered
  3. Prepare 2 lbs. of small red potatoes
  4. Take 8 ears sweet corn
  5. Make ready 1 onion…cut in half
  6. Prepare 4 Tblsp salt
  7. Get 2 garlic gloves
  8. Take 2 packs sausage (preferably HillShire)
  9. Take cayenne pepper (optional)
  10. Make ready bag Crab boil liquid or
Instructions to make Winter Shrimp Crab Boil:
  1. Boil water, spices, salt, lemon, and onions for 5 minutes
  2. Add crab legs…boil 5 minutes
  3. Add sausage and red potatoes and corn…boil 10 minutes
  4. Add shrimp…boil 5 minutes
  5. Let sit so season can soak…at least 15-30 minutes…the longer it sits, the more flavor you'll experience…
  6. Enjoy!!!

Steps to make Beef_nihari:
  1. Heat oil in a large skillet. Fry the chopped onion till it turns dark brown. drain the oil
  2. In the same skillet add the beef, garam masala powder, ginger garlic paste, salt, red chilli powder, a stick of cinnamon and paprika powder (All grind in powder form) add with beef.. Fry until the oil forms a separate layer on top.
  3. Add red crushed red chilli, turmeric powder salt & mix well then cover for 5 mins. Then add kasuri methi, garam masala, green chilli mix well & cover for 5 mins
  4. Then add the nutmeg cardamoms, fennel seeds, Aniseeds,ginger powder and water. Cook until the beef tenders. Mix the corn flour in milk and pour the mixture in the gravy, stirring continuously.
  5. Finally add cumin & coriander powder mix well cook reduce the flame to a simmer and cook for another 10 minutes. then switch off the flame
  6. Serve in a dish and garnish with chopped coriander, brown onions and chopped ginger, lastly sprinkle the garam masala on top and serve.

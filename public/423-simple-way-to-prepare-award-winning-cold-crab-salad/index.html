The ingredients needed to make Cold Crab Salad:
  1. Prepare 8 oz Crab Classic - Imitation Crab Leg Style
  2. Prepare 3 green onion
  3. Take 3 stick celery
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp lemon juice
  6. Take 2 dash onion powder
  7. Get 3 tbsp fresh cracked pepper
  8. Make ready 1 box Keebler Club Crackers

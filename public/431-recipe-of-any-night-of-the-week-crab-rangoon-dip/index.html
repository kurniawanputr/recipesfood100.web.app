The ingredients needed to make crab Rangoon dip:
  1. Take dips
  2. Make ready 2 cup crab meat
  3. Prepare 16 oz cream cheese soft
  4. Take 1/2 cup sour cream
  5. Get 4 green onions chopped
  6. Take 1 1/2 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp powered sugar

Instructions to make crab Rangoon dip:
  1. chop crab and green onion in food processor
  2. in bowl add all ingredients and mix well
  3. bake for 30 minutes at 350°F

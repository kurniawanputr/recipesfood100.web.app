The ingredients needed to make Pasta:
  1. Take 1 cup Boiled Pasta
  2. Take 1 Chopped onion
  3. Make ready 1 teaspoon Chopped garlic
  4. Prepare 1 Chopped tomato
  5. Get Chopped spring onions 3 strings
  6. Take to taste Salt
  7. Get 1 teaspoon Sugar
  8. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon Chilli flakes
  9. Get 1 tsp soya sauce
  10. Make ready 1 tsp Tomato sauce
  11. Prepare 2 tablespoons Oil

Instructions to make Pasta:

  2. Add salt and keep stirring

  4. Add the sauces and a teaspoon of sugar. Mix well
  5. Sprinkle chilli flakes and dish hot.

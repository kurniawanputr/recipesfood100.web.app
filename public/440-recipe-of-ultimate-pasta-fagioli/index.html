The ingredients needed to make Pasta Fagioli:
  1. Make ready 1 tbsp. olive oil
  2. Get 2 tbs. garlic
  3. Prepare 3-6 ribs celery sliced
  4. Prepare 3-4 Carrots peeled and diced
  5. Make ready 1 sweet onion finely chopped
  6. Get 1/3 cup parsley
  7. Make ready 2 cans Great Northern Beans
  8. Make ready 1 (28 oz.) can San Marzano tomatoes
  9. Make ready 1/2 carton chicken or vegetable broth
  10. Make ready 2 cups water
  11. Get 1/2 cup wine of choice
  12. Get 1/2 lb. Ditalini
  13. Make ready Parmesan Cheese for Garnish

Instructions to make Pasta Fagioli:
  1. In a a large pot, Saute oil, garlic, celery, carrots, onion for 10 minutes
  2. Add in beans, tomatoes, parsley, chicken/vegetable broth, water, and wine. Simmer for at least an hour. the longer the better
  3. In a separate pot, boil water to make pasta. Make pasta. Drain water and keep pasta aside.
  4. Serve soup over pasta.

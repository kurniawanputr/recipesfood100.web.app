The ingredients needed to make Garden pasta:
  1. Get 1 jar (24 to 26 ounces) spicy tomato basil pasta sauce
  2. Make ready 2 cups (5 ounces) uncooked Bowtie pasta
  3. Prepare 1 can (about 14 ounces) stewed tomatoes
  4. Prepare 1 cup small broccoli florets
  5. Prepare 1 cup finely diced yellow squash or zucchini
  6. Take 1/2 cup water
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil or tarragon

Steps to make Garden pasta:
  1. Coat inside of crock-pot slow cooker with nonstick cooking spray. Combine pasta sauce,pasta,tomatoes,broccoli,yellow squash and water in crock-pot slow cooker; mix well.

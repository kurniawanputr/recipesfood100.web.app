The ingredients needed to make Pizza Pasta:
  1. Prepare 1 large box pasta (you choose)
  2. Make ready 2 large jars pizza sauce
  3. Get 1 lb. hamburger
  4. Take 1 yellow onion, diced
  5. Prepare 1 Can black olives, sliced
  6. Get 1 Tablespoon onion powder
  7. Prepare 1 Tablespoon garlic powder
  8. Take to taste salt and black pepper,
  9. Make ready 1 Pkg. sliced pepperoni, diced
  10. Prepare to taste grated parmesan / romano cheese
Steps to make Pizza Pasta:
  1. In a large skillet add the hamburger, onion, pepperoni, onion and garlic powders, and Salt and pepper. Fry til Hamburger is no longer pink; drain grease and set meat mixture aside.
  2. Bring a large pot half full of water to a boil. Add the pasta, stirring occasionally. Continue to boil til pasta is tender; drain. Add noodles and meat mixture to large pot along with the olives and sauces. Mix well. To Serve top with Parmesan and/or Romano Cheese and Enjoy ðŸ¥°

The ingredients needed to make Beetroot pasta:
  1. Take 1 cup boiled rotini pasta
  2. Take 1 cup diced onion
  3. Take 1 cup diced beetroot
  4. Make ready 1 cup diced tomato
  5. Make ready 1 cup diced green onion
  6. Get 1 cup grated carrots
  7. Get 1 tablespoon salt
  8. Take 1 tsp black pepper
  9. Take 1 tablespoon garam masala
  10. Get 1 tablespoon chaat masala
  11. Prepare 1 tablespoon red chilli powder
  12. Take 2 tablespoons ketchup
  13. Take 2 tablespoons dark soy sauce

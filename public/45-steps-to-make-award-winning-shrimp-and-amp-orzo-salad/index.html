The ingredients needed to make Shrimp & Orzo Salad:
  1. Take 8 oz orzo pasta
  2. Take 1 lb large shrimp (about 20)
  3. Make ready Juice & zest from 1 lemon
  4. Get 2 tsp Dijon or whole grain mustard
  5. Take 1/4 cup olive oil
  6. Take 1/4 cup finely minced dill
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp finely minced shallot

Instructions to make Shrimp & Orzo Salad:
  1. Cook orzo according to package instructions, boiling for 9-10 minutes but add shrimp in the last 2 minutes to cook.
  2. In a large bow that you will serve in, combine oil, mustard, shallot and dill.
  3. Drain pasta and shrimp and add to mixing bowl. Stir well to combine. Serve at any temperature.

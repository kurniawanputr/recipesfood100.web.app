The ingredients needed to make Creamy Pasta:
  1. Take 50-60 gr spaghetti or fettucini
  2. Get pieces Bacon or sausage or other meat to your taste, cook and cut to small
  3. Prepare 150 ml cooking cream
  4. Prepare 1 clove garlic
  5. Get 400 ml water
  6. Get 1 tbsp olive oil
  7. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon salt
  8. Make ready Pepper
Steps to make Creamy Pasta:
  1. Boil the spaghetti in the water. Add 1/4 teaspoon of salt and stir well. Cook for 8-10 mins until the spaghetti turns soft and set aside.
  2. Chop the garlic. Heat the pan with olive oil and saute the garlic. Turn the heat to medium low.
  3. Add the spaghetti with the leftover water. Add 1/4 teaspoon salt and pepper. Cook for 1-2 mins or until the water become dry.
  4. Add the meat into the pan. Pour the cooking cream. Cook them for another 3-4 mins until the cream boils.
  5. Serve the spaghetti. You can add some parsley or ground blackpepper if you like.

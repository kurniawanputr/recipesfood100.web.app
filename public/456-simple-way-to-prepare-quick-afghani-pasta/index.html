The ingredients needed to make Afghani Pasta:
  1. Get 1 Tbsp turmeric
  2. Take 1 Tbsp salt
  3. Take 1/5 Tbsp black peppers
  4. Prepare 1/5 Tbsp red chilli peppers
  5. Make ready 2 tomatoes
  6. Prepare 1 onion
  7. Make ready 4 garlic
  8. Get 3 potatoes
  9. Get 1 chilli pepper
Steps to make Afghani Pasta:
  1. Peer the onion and chop it in a very small pieces and 2 tomatoes and chop them all. Peer the potatoes and thinly slice them.
  2. And Onion in oil and add the potatoes and cook them for 10 mints after that add garlic and pepper and mix them all together and add the chop tomatoes and add the spices and mix them all together and cook them for 10 mints.

Steps to make Spaghetti pasta:
  1. In a pot of water, boil with salt and oil then add the spaghetti. This will add flavor to the pasta and keep it from sticking together. Once boiling, turn of heat. Let it sit in the water until al dente. Drain and set pasta aside.
  2. In the same pot, add butter. Saute garlic and onion.
  3. Add the ground pork. Saute until almost dry. Add in the pork cubes while stirring.
  4. Toss in the hotdogs. Stir for another 5mins.
  5. Add the spaghetti sauce. Add a cup of water. Let it boil under medium heat.
  6. Season with salt pepper.
  7. Serve. Shred some cheese on top. Enjoy üòä

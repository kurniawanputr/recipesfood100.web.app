The ingredients needed to make Tuna Pasta:
  1. Prepare 1 can tuna in vegetable oil
  2. Take 1 whole onion chopped
  3. Make ready 5 gloves of garlic chopped
  4. Take 1/4 cup olive oil
  5. Prepare 1 whole green bell pepper chopped
  6. Prepare 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  7. Take 1/4 cup parsley chopped
  8. Take 100 grams spaghetti pasta
  9. Make ready to taste Salt and Pepper
Steps to make Tuna Pasta:
  1. Sauté onions and garlic in olive oil
  2. Add tuna and sauté for 5 mins
  3. Add chopped green bell pepper
  4. Cook pasta according to package instructions
  5. Drain pasta and mix the tuna put chopped parsley and top with grated cheddar cheese.

The ingredients needed to make Sardines Pasta:
  1. Make ready Cooking oil/Olive Oil
  2. Take 1 large Red Onion
  3. Take 1/2 cup Bread Crumbs
  4. Prepare 5 cloves garlic
  5. Get 1 medium green bell pepper, diced
  6. Prepare 2 large tomatoes thinly sliced
  7. Prepare 1 can spanish sardines
  8. Make ready 175 grams spaghetti pasta
  9. Prepare to taste Salt and ground pepper
  10. Get Fresh parsley

Steps to make Sardines Pasta:
  1. Saute Garlic and Bread Crumbs in Olive Oil, add a little salt, until slightly brown. Set aside
  2. Saute garlic, then onion until translucent
  3. Add tomato until crushed.
  4. Add oil from sardines. (Remove whole peppers included in can). Cover. Let tomatoes blend with oil.
  5. Wait until tomatoes are soft and fully crushed.
  6. Add Sardines. Saute for 5 minutes. Do not cover.
  7. Add salt and pepper.
  8. Add cooked pasta. Mix well.
  9. Add Garlic and Bread Crumbs mixture
  10. Add crushed parsley and serve.

The ingredients needed to make Pasta:
  1. Get 1 cup pasta, fusilli
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup red bell pepper sliced
  3. Take 1/4 cup yellow bell pepper sliced
  4. Make ready 1/4 capsicum sliced
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup carrot sliced
  6. Prepare 1 onion chopped
  7. Get 1 tbsp garlic chopped
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp ginger chopped
  9. Make ready 1 tsp soya sauce
  10. Take 1 tsp chilli sauce
  11. Make ready 1 tsp vinegar
  12. Prepare 1 tsp sugar
  13. Take 2 tsp cornflour
  14. Make ready 1 tsp pepper powder
  15. Make ready Oil salt

Instructions to make Pasta:
  1. Boil water in a pan. Add one tsp oil and salt in it. Then add pasta. Cook for 8 minutes or the pasta should be 80 percent cooked. Once the pasta is cooked drain the water.
  2. Now in a pan heat 2 tbsp of oil. Add chopped garlic and ginger. After few second add chopped onion. Saute for two minutes.
  3. Now add all the bell peppers and carrot. Cook for 2-3 minutes. Add salt chilli sauce, pepper powder, soya sauce, sugar and vinegar. Cook for few second.
  4. Now add pasta and mix it well. Cook for 2-3 minutes on medium flame. Add one tbsp of cornflour in a bowl. Add water. Mix well. Cook for 1-2 minutes. Then serve.

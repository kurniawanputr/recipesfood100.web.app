Steps to make Pasta:
  1. First of all boil half packet pasta add a teaspoon of oil while boiling so that it will not get stick. Keep them aside.
  2. Put a kadai on flame add oil, put chopped ginger, garlic and chill and crackle it then put onion and other vegetable.
  3. Add salt, red chilli flakes and sauces and boiled pasta and cook it for a minute.
  4. Add seasoning your pasta is ready and serve it hot.

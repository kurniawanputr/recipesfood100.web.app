The ingredients needed to make Christmas Pasta:
  1. Make ready 16 oz. Penne
  2. Take 1 lb ground beef
  3. Take 1 green bell pepper diced
  4. Take 1 red bell pepper diced
  5. Take 1 medium onion diced
  6. Take 1 pint heavy whipping cream
  7. Get 8 oz. Shredded velveeta
Steps to make Christmas Pasta:
  1. Cook pasta according to directions on the box
  2. Dice peppers and onion while pasta is cooking
  3. Cook veggies and ground meat together (I like to add some meat seasoning for extra flavor but this is optional).
  4. Cook until pink is no longer visible in ground meat and drain
  5. Combine velveeta and heavy whipping cream in a large pot
  6. Heat, stirring constantly, until mixture is boiling.
  7. Turn off the flame and stir in ground meat mixture.
  8. Stir in cooked pasta last.
  9. Serve on a festive plate to maximize effect.

Steps to make Pasta:
  1. In a large kadai boil 6 cup water and 1 tbsp salt. - once the water comes to a boil, add penne pasta. Boil for 10 mins. Drain off the pasta and keep aside.
  2. In a frying pan, heat oil add - Cumin seeds,Onion,capsicum,tomato and sweet corn saute it.
  3. Now add salt,red chilli powder,black pepper powder and saute it.
  4. Now add red chilli sauce,tomato sauce,malai and mix well.
  5. Now add pasta and mix well. Serve it.

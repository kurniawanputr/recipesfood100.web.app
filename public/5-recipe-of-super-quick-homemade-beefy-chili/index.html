The ingredients needed to make Beefy Chili:
  1. Take 2 lb lean ground beef
  2. Prepare 1 pablano pepper diced
  3. Prepare 1 med yellow onion diced
  4. Get 2 (14.5 oz) cans diced fire roasted tomatoes
  5. Take 1 (6 oz) can tomato paste
  6. Make ready 2 tsp need better than bouillon
  7. Make ready 1 (12 oz) bottle of dark beer or ipa
  8. Take 1 1/2 tbsp chili powder
  9. Prepare 11/2 tbsp smoked paprika
  10. Get 1 1/2 tsp onion powder
  11. Take 1 tsp garlic powder
  12. Get 1 tsp cumin
  13. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp salt
  14. Take 1 tsp salt
  15. Prepare 4-6 cloves garlic minced
  16. Get 2 tbsp oil
  17. Get Garnishes
  18. Get chopped cilantro
  19. Prepare diced onion
  20. Prepare shredded cabbage
  21. Make ready lime wedges
  22. Get sour cream
  23. Make ready cheese
Instructions to make Beefy Chili:
  1. Heat the 2 tbsp oil in a 4.5qt or larger Dutch Oven over med heat. Add onions and peppers and cook for about 2 minutes.
  2. Add ground beef and break up with a wooden spoon. cook until no longer pink. drain grease if you did not use a lean beef. add minced garlic and stir for 1 min more.
  3. Add all your spices and tomato paste. stir to incorporate and cook for about 2 minutes. add better than bouillon and stir until dissolved.
  4. Add bottle of beer and sommer for 2 minutes.
  5. Add both cans of diced tomatoes.
  6. Simmer covered for 1 hour on low heat.
  7. Serve and top with favorite garnishes.

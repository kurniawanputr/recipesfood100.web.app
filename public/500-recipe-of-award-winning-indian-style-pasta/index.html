  5. Get 2 Eggs
  6. Get Oil
  7. Get Salt as per taste
  8. Make ready Red chilli powder
  9. Make ready Tomato sauce/tomato paste / pasta sauce

Instructions to make INDIAN style pasta:
  1. Boil pasta and drain all the water with strainer
  2. Chop onion tomato in to small pieces
  3. In pan take some oil then chopped onion after it turns into brown put tomatoes salt Ginger/ garlic paste some red chilli powder mix it well and wait for 5mints
  4. Take eggs put in the same pan fry and mix after that some pasta sauce or tomato paste cook it for 2mints
  5. Put pasta in the pan mix everything and garnish with some chopped corrindor.

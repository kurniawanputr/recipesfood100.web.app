Instructions to make Pasta:
  1. Take pan add water and little bit salt boil it  Then add your pasta cook it well
  2. Then take out and wash it with cold water and add some oil on pasta.
  3. Take another pan add Butter/oil then add garlic Saute well add chilli onion saute it till it comes to pink or colour change. Add maggi masala.
  4. Then add Salt (Remember you are using mayonnaise,butter, cheese so already have salt
  5. Then add Bell pepper saute well till it become soft
  6. Add mushroom, sweet corn
  7. Add kali mirch Mix it well
  8. Now add mayonnaise,cheese spread mix well now add chilli flakes and oregano
  9. Give it a nice stir for 2 min
  10. Now Add pasta toss well for 3 min
  11. Serve it in plate with oregano
  12. At last you can add any vegetable or boil chicken whatever your favourite.. you can chnge the ratio of mayonnaise cheese butter also

The ingredients needed to make Lemon-Butter Pasta:
  1. Prepare Pasta
  2. Get Handful (ca. 4oz) pasta
  3. Take Sauce
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp butter
  5. Take 1 small scoop minced garlic
  6. Take 1/2 lemon, the juice
  7. Prepare splash chicken broth (optional)
  8. Prepare Few shakes of basil
  9. Get Parmesan (however cheesy you want!)
Steps to make Lemon-Butter Pasta:
  1. Boil water. Add pasta. Cook for 10 min.
  2. In a small frying pan, add butter, garlic, and lemon juice and cook on high. Then turn down and add remaining ingredients. (Chicken broth, basil, Parmesan)
  3. Top with Parmesan! Add some red pepper flakes if you’re feeling spicy!

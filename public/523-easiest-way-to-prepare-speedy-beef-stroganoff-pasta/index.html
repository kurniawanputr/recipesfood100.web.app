The ingredients needed to make Beef stroganoff pasta:
  1. Take 1 1/2 pounds beef sirloin steak, 1/2 inch thick
  2. Get 2 cups sliced button mushrooms
  3. Make ready 2 chopped medium size onions
  4. Get 1 chopped clove garlic
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 cup beef broth
  6. Make ready 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  7. Take 1/4 cup all purpose flour
  8. Prepare 1 1/2 cups sour cream
  9. Get 3 cups cooked egg noodles
  10. Get to taste Salt and pepper
  11. Make ready Chopped parsley for garnishing

Instructions to make Beef stroganoff pasta:
  1. Sauté onions, garlic and mushrooms in butter until cooked and set aside
  2. Sauté beef in the same pan you used for the onions. Put pepper, salt and soy sauce until golden brown. Pour beef broth and cook for 20 minutes.
  3. Put sour cream and flour. Simmer for five minutes and pour onion mixture.
  4. Cook egg noodles according to package. Garnish with chopped parsley.

The ingredients needed to make Pasta:
  1. Take 1 cup Boiled pasta
  2. Take 1 Onion

  6. Take 1/2 teaspoon Red chilli powder
  7. Make ready 1 spoon Sauce
  8. Take 1 Capsicum

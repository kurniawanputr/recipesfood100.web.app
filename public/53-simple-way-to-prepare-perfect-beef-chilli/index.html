The ingredients needed to make Beef Chilli:
  1. Make ready Beef: 1-1.5 lbs of beef
  2. Make ready Onions: 1 onions
  3. Take Garlic: 4 gloves or about 2 tablespoons
  4. Get Olive Oil: tablespoons olive oil
  5. Get Cumin: 1 1/2 tablespoons of cumin
  6. Take Brown Sugar: 1 1/2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  7. Prepare Green Bell Pepper: 1/2 1/2 tablespoons green bell pepper
  8. Get Chilli Powder: 1 1/2 tablespoons of chilli powder
  9. Prepare Oregano: 2 teaspoons of oregano
  10. Take Thyme: 2 teaspoons of thyme
  11. Get Paprika: 2 teaspoons of paprika
  12. Prepare Salt: 2 teaspoons of salt
  13. Take Pepper: 1 teaspoons of pepper
  14. Take Cayenne Pepper: 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  15. Get Tomatoes: 1 can of diced tomatoes
  16. Get Tomato Sauce: 1 can of tomato sauce
  17. Get Tomato Paste: 2 1/2 tablespoon of tomato paste
  18. Make ready Chicken Broth: 2 cups of chicken broth
  19. Take Kidney Beans: 1 can kidney beans or mixed beans
  20. Make ready Toppings
  21. Get Cheese (of your choice)
  22. Prepare Cilantro
  23. Take Jalapeños: stems removed and minced
  24. Prepare Fritos
  25. Prepare Sour Cream
  26. Make ready Green Onion
Steps to make Beef Chilli:
  1. Cook meat in medium pan with oil until broken up and cooked through
  2. Add the onion to the meat and add S&P until soft. Then add garlic bell peppers. Then drain the fat and grease.
  3. Add in the beans, seasonings, dice tomoatoes, with sauce, and broth,
  4. Mix and cook for about 20 minutes. Taste and season to liking.

The ingredients needed to make Pasta:
  1. Get 1 cup pasta
  2. Prepare 3 onion
  3. Get 4 tomatoes
  4. Make ready 1 capsicum chopped
  5. Prepare 2 tsp olives
  6. Prepare 5-6 garlic cloves
  7. Prepare To taste salt
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp black pepper Powder
  10. Prepare As needed cheese
  11. Get As needed oil
  12. Prepare 2 tsp chilli flakes

Steps to make Pasta:
  1. In a large vessel cook penned pasta with some water and salt and oil.Cook for 10 mints till they are soft.
  2. Now drain the pasta and keep aside.
  3. Boiled tomato and whole chilli for 5 mints and blend to smooth puree.
  4. In a pan heat the butter and add garlic,onion, capsicum and saute it.
  5. Add tomato puree and saute for 5 mints. Add pepper powder,salt,tomato sauce,olives and mix well.
  6. Add boiled pasta,cover and cook for 5 mints.Red sauce pasta is ready and garnishing with chilli flakes.
  7. Ready to serve.
  8. Serve hot.

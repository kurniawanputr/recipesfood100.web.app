The ingredients needed to make Summertime Pasta Salad:
  1. Take 1 box (16 ounce) your favorite pasta
  2. Get Salt for boiling water
  3. Take 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  4. Make ready 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  5. Prepare 1 tablespoon parsley flakes
  6. Prepare 1 zucchini, peeled and cut into cubes
  7. Take 1/2 red onion, chopped
  8. Make ready 1 red pepper, chopped
  9. Get 1 can (4.25 ounce) black olives
  10. Get 1 bottle (16 ounce) Greek dressing
Steps to make Summertime Pasta Salad:
  1. Bring pasta to a boil….. Don’t forget to salt your water!!
  2. Drain pasta, rinse in cold water and return to pot after draining thoroughly……
  3. Add to pasta your black pepper, garlic powder, parsley flakes, zucchini, red onion, red pepper, black olives and Greek dressing, stir thoroughly……
  4. Cover and refrigerate for several hours or overnight, stirring occasionally…..Serve and enjoy 😉!

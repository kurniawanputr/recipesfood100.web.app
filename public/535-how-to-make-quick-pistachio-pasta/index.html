The ingredients needed to make Pistachio Pasta:
  1. Make ready 1 cup cooked pasta (any kind)
  2. Get 250 g breast chicken
  3. Make ready 1 small sweet onion/white onion, chopped
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp olive oil
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  6. Get 2 tbsp feta cheese
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp brown sugar
  8. Get 4 tbsp water
  9. Make ready to taste Salt and black pepper
  10. Get Pistacio puree; in food processor
  11. Get 3 tbsp pistachio ground
  12. Make ready Parsley
  13. Get leaves Mint
  14. Get 6 tbsp olive oil
  15. Make ready 3 gloves garlic
  16. Prepare to taste Salt and black pepper
  17. Prepare Garnish:
  18. Take 1 tbsp coconut flakes
  19. Prepare 1 tbsp pistachio powder
Steps to make Pistachio Pasta:
  1. In cooking pot,heat olive oil over meduim heaat then add onion then add chicken. Keep it till the chicken dry.
  2. Then add balsamic vinegar,salt and black pepper,feta cheese and sugar then cook for a minute.
  3. Then add pistacio puree mix well then add pasta cooked. And cook for 5 minutes.

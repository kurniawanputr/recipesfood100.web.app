The ingredients needed to make Chicken Pesto Pasta:
  1. Get Chicken
  2. Get Pesto
  3. Prepare Bowtie Noodles
  4. Get Lemon Juice
  5. Get Broccoli
  6. Make ready Salt
  7. Get Pepper
  8. Get Italian Seasoning
  9. Get Parsley Flakes
  10. Prepare Milk
  11. Take Butter
Steps to make Chicken Pesto Pasta:
  1. Boil water in a pot then add the noodles, cook until soft.
  2. Chop up broccoli and steam until noodles are done.
  3. Add butter, Italian seasoning, parsley flakes, salt and pepper to the broccoli.
  4. Mix noodles in with the broccoli.
  5. Add lemon juice, chopped up chicken, butter, pesto and a bit of milk and mix together.
  6. Lastly add a bit more salt, pepper, Italian seasoning and parsley flakes.

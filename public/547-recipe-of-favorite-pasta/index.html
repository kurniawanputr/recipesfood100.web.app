The ingredients needed to make Pasta:

  3. Get 1/4 tsp black pepper powder

Instructions to make Pasta:
  1. In a pan add oil.
  2. Now add capsicum and saute for a minute. Then add onion and saute again.
  3. Now add boiled pasta.
  4. Now add all the spices and tomato sauce.
  5. Mix well and cook for 1 minute
  6. Pasta is ready to serve.
  7. Serve hot

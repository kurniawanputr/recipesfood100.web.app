The ingredients needed to make Pasta:
  1. Take 1 cup pasta boiled
  2. Make ready 1 onion sliced
  3. Get 1/4 cup capsicum sliced
  4. Get 1 Tomato sliced
  5. Take 3 tsp oil
  6. Prepare to taste salt and red chilli powder
  7. Prepare 3 tsp tomato sauce
  8. Get 1 tsp vinegar
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp green chilli sauce

Instructions to make Pasta:
  1. In a pan add oil and onion.saute it for five minutes.
  2. Now add tomato and capsicum.mix well.cook again for five minutes.
  3. Now add paste and all spices.mix well.
  4. Add both the sauces and Vinegar.mix well.cook for a minute.
  5. Pasta is ready to serve.

The ingredients needed to make Simple Vegetarian Pasta:
  1. Get 3 servings of any whole grain pasta
  2. Prepare 2 cloves minced garlic
  3. Take 1/2 diced onion
  4. Make ready 1 tomato
  5. Prepare 3 cups baby spinach
  6. Prepare 3/4 cup grated Parmesan
  7. Make ready Pinch salt
  8. Make ready Pinch pepper
  9. Take 1 tbsp olive oil
  10. Make ready 1 stick butter
Instructions to make Simple Vegetarian Pasta:
  1. Prepare pasta from directions on package. Add salt and olive oil to the water.
  2. Add some butter to pan on medium heat. Add garlic to get that aroma going (~1 min). Then add onions and spinach and some more of the butter. Stir occasionally for about 10 minutes.
  3. Put pasta on low heat when done. Stir in some butter to the pasta. Then parmesan. Keep stirring. Once melted, add the onion/garlic/spinach mix.
  4. Top with chopped tomato and fresh parm chef’s kiss

The ingredients needed to make Creamy Dijon Pasta:
  1. Get Shells
  2. Prepare Mushrooms
  3. Get Spinach
  4. Prepare 1/2 tbsp Dijon sauce
  5. Get 1/2 cup Coconut cream
  6. Take 2 tbsp Dijon mustard
  7. Take 1 tsp garlic powder
  8. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  9. Get 1/2 tsp pepper
  10. Get (Fresh) basil
  11. Get Red pepper flakes
Instructions to make Creamy Dijon Pasta:
  1. Boil shells and sauté mushrooms + spinach with oil
  2. Add Dijon and coconut cream. Stir. Then add everything else.
  3. Add basil and red pepper flakes last.

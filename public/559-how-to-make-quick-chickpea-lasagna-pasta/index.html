The ingredients needed to make Chickpea Lasagna Pasta:
  1. Take 1 box bowtie noodles
  2. Make ready 1 can Chickpeas
  3. Get 1 can diced tomatoes (drained)
  4. Take 1 can tomato sauce
  5. Make ready 1 can sliced olives (optional)
  6. Prepare 2 Tbs butter
  7. Make ready 1 cup shredded cheese
  8. Take 1/4 cup parmesan cheese
  9. Make ready 1/2 chopped onion
  10. Get 3 cloves garlic
  11. Get 1/2 Tbs Italian herbs
  12. Take 1 tsp salt
  13. Prepare 1/2 Tbs onion powder
  14. Take 1/2 Tbs garlic powder
  15. Make ready 1/2 Tbs Italian parsley
Instructions to make Chickpea Lasagna Pasta:
  1. Boil Bowtie noodles as directed on box. As noodles are cooking add butter, garlic and onions into a shallow sauce pan. Saute until onions are translucent in color.
  2. Drain and rise your chickpeas and add directly into the sauce pan. Then add your can of olives (optional), and diced tomatoes. Let cook on medium heat for 5 minutes, stirring frequently.
  3. Add in your favorite tomato sauce. Add in your 1/4 cup parmesan cheese and stir until well combined. Cover and let simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Drain pasta and remove from heat. Combine pasta with your sauce. Add in 1 cup of shredded cheese. Mix together and let sit 5 minutes covered before serving.

The ingredients needed to make Garlic Butter Shrimp:
  1. Get 1 kilo shrimps
  2. Prepare 5 cloves garlic (minced)
  3. Take 2 tbsp butter
  4. Take 3-4 calamansi or 1 pc lemon
  5. Get 1 tsp sugar
  6. Take 1 tbsp oyster sauce
  7. Take Salt & pepper
  8. Prepare Maggie magic sarap
  9. Prepare Chilli flakes (optional)
  10. Prepare Ketchup & hot sauce (optional)
Steps to make Garlic Butter Shrimp:
  1. Marinate shrimp in calamansi, salt, pepper and Maggie Magic Sarap
  2. Put in butter in pan until it melts. The fry the garlic.
  3. Put in the shrimp. One side first. Then flip the other side until orange.
  4. Put in a little of the marinade, you can add ketchup or hot sauce.
  5. Season with sugar, salt & pepper.

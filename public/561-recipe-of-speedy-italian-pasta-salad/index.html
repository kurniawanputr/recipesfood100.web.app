The ingredients needed to make Italian Pasta Salad:
  1. Prepare Half yellow bell pepper, diced
  2. Get Half orange bell pepper, diced
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup cucumbers, diced
  4. Take 1/3 cup red onion, diced
  5. Get 8 oz grape tomatoes, halved
  6. Make ready 1 cup Italian style finely shredded cheese
  7. Take 1 (16 oz) bag of tri-color rotini pasta
  8. Make ready 1 cup Italian dressing
  9. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper
  10. Prepare 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
Instructions to make Italian Pasta Salad:
  1. In a large pot, bring water to a boil and cook noodles according to directions on package
  2. Drain noodles and rinse with cold water
  3. Mix in all vegetables, seasonings, cheese, and dressing. Refrigerate leftovers!

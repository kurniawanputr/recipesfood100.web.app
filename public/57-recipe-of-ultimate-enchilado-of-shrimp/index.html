The ingredients needed to make Enchilado of Shrimp:
  1. Get 1 - lbs Raw & and deveined shrimp
  2. Get 1 - cup white cooking wine
  3. Take 1/4 onion diced
  4. Make ready 1/4 red bell pepper diced
  5. Get 2 - garlic cloves minced
  6. Make ready Pinch cumin
  7. Take 1 tsp olive oil
  8. Get 1 - small can tomato sauce
  9. Prepare Splash soy sauce
  10. Get to taste Salt and pepper
  11. Take Small splash hot sauce

The ingredients needed to make Pasta Primavera:
  1. Prepare 16 oz taglitelli or bowties pasta
  2. Get 8 asparagus
  3. Prepare 2 carrots
  4. Prepare 8 green beans
  5. Prepare 1 small zucchini
  6. Take 8 oz heavy cream
  7. Take 1 tbsp. Butter
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp. Olive oil
  9. Make ready parsley
  10. Take Parmigano reggiano cheese
  11. Get salt and pepper
  12. Get garlic powder
Instructions to make Pasta Primavera:
  1. Steam vegetables carrots, string beans and asparagus for a few minutes don't overcook. (First steam asparagus separately whole after chopping off woody ends) then put on side in a drainer and run cold water to stop cooking. Do not steam zucchini.
  2. Then add 1tbs. olive oil to large skillet and saute vegetables over medium heat. In a large pot boil water for pasta. Do not overcook pasta it should be aldente. Add 8oz. heavy cream and butter to pan stirring constantly until well mixed. Add garlic powder and add pasta and cheese, serve immediately. Season with salt and pepper to your liking. Enjoy!
  3. Preparation and cooking time approx. 30 - 45min.

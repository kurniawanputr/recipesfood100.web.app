The ingredients needed to make Alkaline Tomato Basil Pasta:
  1. Get 1/4 Cup Walnuts
  2. Get 1/4 Cup Bella Mushrooms
  3. Get 6 Roma Tomatoes, cut in halves
  4. Take 1-2 Cups Spelt Pasta
  5. Prepare 1/2 Cup Green Bell Pepper, diced
  6. Get 1/2 Cup Green Onions, chopped
  7. Take 1 Large Shallot Clove, chopped
  8. Take 1/2 Teaspoon Sea Salt (as desired)
  9. Prepare 1/2 Tablespoon Oregano (as desired)
  10. Make ready 1/2 Tablespoon Basil (as desired)
  11. Take 2 Bay Leaves
  12. Make ready 1/2 Tablespoon Onion Powder (as desired)
  13. Get 1/4 Teaspoon Cayenne Powder (as desired)
  14. Get 2 Tablespoons Grapeseed Oil
Instructions to make Alkaline Tomato Basil Pasta:
  1. Boil walnuts (removes the bitterness from the walnuts).
  2. Ground walnuts in food processor.
  3. Rinse Mushrooms and pay dry.
  4. Ground mushrooms in food processor.
  5. Coat sauce pan with Grapeseed Oil.
  6. Add seasonings to heated oil: oregano, basil, bay leaves and onion power.
  7. Add shallots, green onions and green bell pepper to sauce pan.
  8. Add roma tomatoes to sauce pan, sea salt and cayenne pepper(Roast for 5-10 minutes on stove, uncovered).
  9. Cover sauce pan for 15-20 minutes.
  10. Add ground walnuts and ground mushroom.
  11. Simmer spaghetti sauce.
  12. Boil spelt pasta in vegetable broth. Drain and rinse pasta. Add pasta to spaghetti sauce

The ingredients needed to make Pasta:
  1. Get 1 cup boiled pasta
  2. Take 2 chopped onions
  3. Take 2 chopped potatoes
  4. Prepare 2 chopped tomatoes
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup peas
  6. Get 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  7. Get 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  8. Get 1/4 tsp turmeric powder
  9. Get To taste salt
  10. Get 2 tbsp Tomato sauce
  11. Prepare 2 tsp vinegar

Instructions to make Pasta:
  1. Heat a pan and put one tablespoon oil in it and add cumin seeds, chopped onion, potato and peas.
  2. As it appears golden in colour add all sauces in it and add boiled pasta.
  3. Mix it well and cook it for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Pasta is ready to serve.

The ingredients needed to make Tuna Pasta Salad:
  1. Get 2 cup pasta cooked
  2. Make ready 2 cans tuna in water
  3. Get 2 cucumber
  4. Make ready 2 tomatoes
  5. Prepare 2 green onion
  6. Prepare Cup chopped peppers yellow red
  7. Take 1/2 cup dry parsley
  8. Get 2 tablespoon dry mint
  9. Take 2 tablespoon seeds or chopped walnuts
  10. Prepare Can beats
  11. Prepare 3 tablespoon your desired dressing I used creamy Greek
Steps to make Tuna Pasta Salad:
  1. Chop all your vegetables
  2. Pasta cook it 2 minutes less what the box say and wash it in cold water
  3. Wash tuna and squeeze
  4. Mix all ingredients add dressing and serve cold

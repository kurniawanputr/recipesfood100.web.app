The ingredients needed to make PastaCrunch:
  1. Make ready 1.5 cup penne pasta
  2. Prepare 1 cup heavy cream
  3. Take 1/2 cup rice flour
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup cornflakes
  5. Take 1 tbsp salt
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp cheese powder
  7. Get 1 spoon cheddar cheese
  8. Make ready 1 tbsp tomato sauce
  9. Get 1 tbsp mustard sauce
  10. Take 1 tsp basil
  11. Make ready 1 tsp black pepper
  12. Take 1 tsp orgeano
  13. Take 1 spoon brown bread crumbs
  14. Get 1 chopped onion
  15. Make ready 1 tsp crushed garlic ginger
  16. Take For frying Oil

Steps to make PastaCrunch:
  1. Take pan add 3 cups water let it boil now add pasta let it boil for 10 mins.
  2. Strain it and keep aside.Now take pan add chopped onion crushed garlic ginger saute it.
  3. Now add sauces and pasta let it cook for 5 min.keep aside.
  4. In pan add heavy cream let it boil now add cheese slices,powder orgeano and basil mix it.add black pepper and salt.
  5. Now pour white sauce in pasta and set in container for 15 mins.
  6. Cut in rectangular shape meanwhile take rice flour and water make smooth paste.
  7. Coat all pasta in rice paste then bread crumbs and cornflakes.
  8. Deep fry in hot oil serve with sauce.

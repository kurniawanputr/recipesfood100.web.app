Steps to make Pasta by Me:

  3. Add the tomato sauce and some water, and let them boil
  4. In the end add salt, peper, pepermint and let stir for 5 min.
  5. Boil some water in an pot, add the pasta and let to boil.
  6. When pasta is done, add the pasta in the pan mix well and enjoy.

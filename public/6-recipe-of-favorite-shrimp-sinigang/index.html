The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Sinigang:
  1. Take 10 pcs. Shrimp cleaned
  2. Make ready 44 grams Sinigang sa Sampalok Mix
  3. Get 1 bunch Kangkong (water spinach)
  4. Get 5 pieces okra
  5. Take 1 piece tomato sliced
  6. Take 2 pieces long green pepper (Green finger chilies)
  7. Take 1 piece onion sliced
  8. Make ready 2 quarts water
  9. Prepare 3 tsp Fish Sauce
Instructions to make Shrimp Sinigang:
  1. Boil water in a cooking pot. Add onion and tomato Cover and continue to boil for 8 minutes.
  2. Add shrimp. Cook for 1 minute.
  3. Add Knorr Sinigang sa Sampaloc Recipe Mix. Stir until it dilutes completely. Cover and cook for 3 minutes.
  4. Add long green pepper and okra then cook it for 5 minutes
  5. Put the kangkong stalks into the pot. Season with fish sauce and cook for 1 minute
  6. Serve warm with rice

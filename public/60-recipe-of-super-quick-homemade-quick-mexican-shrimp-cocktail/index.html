The ingredients needed to make Quick Mexican Shrimp Cocktail:
  1. Prepare 1 jar Food Cravings Salsa
  2. Make ready 10 large /12 medium/16 small cooked shrimp
  3. Prepare 1 ripe but firm diced avocado
  4. Take 1 jalapeno seeded and finely chopped
  5. Make ready 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  6. Prepare Juice of 1 fresh lime
  7. Get 2 scallions chopped (white and green part)

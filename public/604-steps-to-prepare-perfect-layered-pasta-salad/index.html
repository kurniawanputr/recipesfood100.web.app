The ingredients needed to make Layered Pasta Salad:
  1. Make ready 3 cups pasta shells, cooked and drained
  2. Take 1 large red onion thinly sliced
  3. Take 1 (10 ounce) pkg frozen peas thawed, drained
  4. Get 6 ounces deli style smoked ham chopped
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup bottled ranch dressing
  7. Get 1 cup shredded cheese (I used mild cheddar)
  8. Prepare 1 cup grape tomatoes halved
Steps to make Layered Pasta Salad:
  1. Layer first four ingredients in 3 qt bowl.
  2. Mix mayonnaise and ranch dressing til blended; spread over salad, sealing to edges. Top with cheese and tomatoes.
  3. Refrigerate several hours. Toss gently before serving.

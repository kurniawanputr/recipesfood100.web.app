The ingredients needed to make Yummy Creamy Mushroom Pasta:
  1. Prepare 100 g fussili pasta
  2. Prepare 100 g champion mushrooms
  3. Make ready 4 tbsp yellow mustard
  4. Get to taste Salt
  5. Prepare Little oil
  6. Make ready 100 g Cerski cheese or any good melting cheese
  7. Make ready 3 cups milk, more if needed

Steps to make Yummy Creamy Mushroom Pasta:
  1. Wash and peel the mushrooms and cut them into smaller pieces, use stems also. Heat some oil in a pan, throw in the mushrooms, salt it and add the mustard. Stir often
  2. When mushrooms get golden, add the milk and then add the pasta. Cook it until pasta is ready
  3. Add generous amount of grated cheese and mix well so it melts well.serve hot

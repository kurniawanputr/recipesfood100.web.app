The ingredients needed to make Italian Deli Pasta Salad:
  1. Take To taste Kosher salt
  2. Make ready 1 pound pasta preferably Penne but any kind works
  3. Take 1/3 cup mayonnaise
  4. Take 3 tablespoons olive oil
  5. Get 3 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  6. Make ready 1 (16 ounce) jar giardiniera, drained and chopped
  7. Take 5 ounces Provolone cheese diced (1 cup)
  8. Take 5 ounces salami, diced (1 cup)
  9. Get 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  10. Make ready 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  11. Make ready To taste Freshly ground pepper

Instructions to make Italian Deli Pasta Salad:
  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook per label directions. Drain and let cool to room temperature.
  2. Mix the mayonnaise, olive oil and red wine vinegar in a large bowl.
  3. Add the pasta and toss well.
  4. Add the giardiniera, provolone, salami. parsley and basil: toss well and season with salt and pepper.

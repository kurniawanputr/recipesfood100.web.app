The ingredients needed to make Creamy Chicken Alfredo Pasta:
  1. Get 1 Cup Chicken (Cut in small Pieces)
  2. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt + ½ tbsp for cooking the pasta
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp pepper powder
  4. Get 1 tsp red chilli powder
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  6. Get 4 cups Water
  7. Get 1 1/2 tbsp Oil
  8. Get 1 1/2 Cups Pasta
  9. Take 3 tbsp Butter
  10. Prepare Garlic 1 tsp (chopped)
  11. Get 1 tsp Ginger (chopped)
  12. Get 1 Cup Fresh cream
  13. Get 1/2 Cup Grated cheese
Steps to make Creamy Chicken Alfredo Pasta:
  1. Marinate 1 Cup of chicken (Cut in small Pieces) with 1 tbsp tomato ketchup, 1 tsp red chilli powder, ½ tsp pepper powder and ¼ tsp salt
  2. Now boil 4 cups of water and add ½ tbsp salt and 1½ tbsp oil mix well and add 1½ cups of Pasta and cook of 8 minutes.
  3. Drain the Pasta and reserve.
  4. Now heat 3 tbsp butter until it melts. Add 1 tsp Ginger chopped and 1 tsp Garlic chopped and Mix.
  5. Now add the marinated Chicken and 1 cup fresh cream and bring to a boil.
  6. Now add the cooked pasta and mix.
  7. Now add half cup of grated cheese and cook well.
  8. Serve and enjoy.

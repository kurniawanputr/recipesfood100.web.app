The ingredients needed to make Tomato pasta:
  1. Take 250 gm boiled pasta
  2. Get 1 large tomato
  3. Get 1 bowl tomato paste
  4. Prepare Chopped cabbage
  5. Make ready 1 large carrot
  6. Prepare 1 tabsp grated ginger and green chilli
  7. Prepare 1 tabsp pasata masala
  8. Make ready 1 bowl boiled Sweetcorn
  9. Get Salt as per taste
  10. Take 2 tabsp oil
  11. Make ready 1 tabsp soya sauce
  12. Take 1 tasp vinegar
  13. Make ready leaves Chopped Coriander

Instructions to make Whetto Pasta:
  1. Cook spaghetti noodles. Reserve a bit of the pasta water. Drain, set aside.
  2. In a pan- add butter, balsamic, spices reserved water and minced garlic. Heat and sauté until butter is melted and ingredients are well combined.
  3. Add cooked taco meat to pan. Warm and combine.
  4. Add pasta, salsa and pico. Toss. Warm and combine.
  5. Remove to a bowl and sprinkle on some Parmesan. Enjoy!

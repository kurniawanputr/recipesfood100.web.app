The ingredients needed to make Beet pasta:
  1. Get 1 pkg frozen beet spirals
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp olive oil
  3. Get 6 oz can of tomato paste
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp aji panca chili paste
  5. Make ready 1 Clove garlic
  6. Take 1 in. Piece of ginger
  7. Take 1 lemon
  8. Make ready 1 tsp. Cumin
  9. Make ready 1 tsp. Cayenne
  10. Take 1 tsp. Salt
  11. Take 1/2 c. Water

Bring to a boil and While the pasta cooks, use your thumbs to rub the skin off the beet(s). Fresh beet pasta is beautiful and delicious. This recipe is egg-free and therefore suitable for vegetarians and vegans. Served with a white wine sauce.

Steps to make Beet pasta:
  1. Heat beet spirals as instructed and set aside
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together tomato and chili pastes with cumin, cayenne, salt, water, 1 tbsp olive oil
  3. Zest lemon, chop ginger and garlic finely
  4. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil with zest, garlic, and ginger
  5. Stir paste into pan on medium heat
  6. Add beet spirals to sauce and heat for 3 minutes.
  7. Enjoy on its own, or over a protein of your choice.

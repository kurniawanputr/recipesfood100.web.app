The ingredients needed to make Taco Shrimp:
  1. Take Shrimp
  2. Get Adobo seasoning salt
  3. Take Parsley
  4. Take Olive garden salad dressing
  5. Prepare Gulden mustard
  6. Get McCormick taco seasoning
Instructions to make Taco Shrimp:
  1. Clean and wash shrimp with lemon.
  2. Pre-heat oven for 400°F Season shrimp with seasonings, mustard, and salad dressing.
  3. Place Seasoned Shrimp in a pan, Cook for 15 minutes.

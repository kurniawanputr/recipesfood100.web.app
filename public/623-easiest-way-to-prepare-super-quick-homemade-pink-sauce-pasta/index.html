The ingredients needed to make Pink sauce pasta:
  1. Prepare Tricolor fusilli pasta
  2. Prepare Water for pasta boil
  3. Get 1 tsp salt for pasta water
  4. Take 500 ml pomi or other tomato sauce brands (no chunks)
  5. Make ready 200 ml or 300ml fresh cream
  6. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  7. Prepare 1 tsp black pepper
  8. Take 1 pinch white pepper
  9. Take 1 tsp onion powder
  10. Prepare 1 pinch garlic powder
  11. Prepare Grated Parmesan
  12. Take 1 handful cut rosemary (optional)
  13. Make ready 1 tsp paprika (optional)
  14. Get 1 tbsp melted butter

Steps to make Pink sauce pasta:
  1. Boil the fusilli with water and salt only (olive oil makes in harder for the sauce to hang on the pasta)
  2. In a bowl, pour the tomato sauce with the salt, black and white pepper, rosemary, onion and garlic powder, and mix, taste the sauce and add spices or rosemary if needed
  3. In a large pan, melt the butter and pour in the tomato sauce, add in the fresh cream and mix till the sauce turns pink, be careful to not over boil it, taste and add in herbs if necessary, add in the fusilli, Parmesan may be added for cheesy taste
  4. Serve hot with grated Parmesan and enjoy

Steps to make Sauce pasta:
  1. Chop the tomato, chop the onion, chop the calcium, chop the carrot, green chilli, coriander seeds, ginger garlic.
  2. Boil the pasta for 10 minutes by adding a bit of oil and salt. Strain the water and pour cold water on it.
  3. Now in a pan, add oil heat it up to add all chopped veggies.
  4. Now add tomato sauce, chilli sauce, red chilli powder, black pepper, salt, coriander seeds and mix it well strain it for 5 minutes.
  5. Pasta is ready to eat.

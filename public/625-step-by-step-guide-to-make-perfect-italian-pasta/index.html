The ingredients needed to make Italian Pasta:
  1. Get 1 big bowl Pasta
  2. Take As required Sweet corn
  3. Make ready 2 Capsicums
  4. Take 2 chopped onions
  5. Prepare 2 tomatoes
  6. Take 2 tbsp ghee
  7. Make ready as required Milk cream
  8. Take As per taste Salt
  9. Get As per taste red chilli Powder
  10. Take As per taste Chilli flakes
  11. Get as per taste Oregano
Steps to make Italian Pasta:
  1. Firstly boil pasta and sweet corn separately.
  2. Put 2 tbps of ghee in Kadai and put cumin, chopped onion and capsicum in kadai. Heat it till onion becomes golden brown
  3. Add tomato puri to the mixture and add species also. Cook it for some time. Then add milk cream, boiled pasta, and sweet corn and mix properly.

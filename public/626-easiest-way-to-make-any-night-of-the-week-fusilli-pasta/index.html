Instructions to make Fusilli pasta:
  1. Add the fusilli pasta to the boiling water which has 1 tsp of salt added to it. Let the pasta cook for 7-8 minutes. Now drain off the water using a sieve. Apply oil to the pasta and keep it aside.
  2. In a pan, take olive oil and heat it. Cook the chopped broccoli and capsicum in the oil. Add salt to taste.
  3. Now add the pasta sauce and little water to it and mix well.
  4. Now add the cheese spread to the pan.
  5. Mix well and after that add the boiled pasta to it. Mix everything properly and let it cook for a while.
  6. Now add red chili flakes and mixed herbs to the paste. Mix well and cook for sometime.
  7. Once done serve the ready hot pasta.

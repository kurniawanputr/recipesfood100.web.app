The ingredients needed to make Quick Mexican Shrimp Cocktail:
  1. Get 1 jar Food Cravings Salsa
  2. Prepare 10 large /12 medium/16 small cooked shrimp
  3. Get 1 ripe but firm diced avocado
  4. Get 1 jalapeno seeded and finely chopped
  5. Get 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  6. Make ready Juice of 1 fresh lime
  7. Prepare 2 scallions chopped (white and green part)
Steps to make Quick Mexican Shrimp Cocktail:
  1. If large shrimp then cut into bite size pieces and combine with salsa and lime juice. Set in fridge while preparing other ingredients. Add avocado, cilantro, scallions, and jalapeno. Refrigerate for 30 minutes if possible but ok to eat now!

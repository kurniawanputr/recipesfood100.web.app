The ingredients needed to make Tomato Pasta:
  1. Get 2 tomatoes
  2. Get 1/2 capsicum
  3. Get 2 onions
  4. Get 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  5. Make ready to taste Salt
  6. Take 1 cup pasta
  7. Make ready 4 tsp Tomato Sauce
  8. Get 2 tsp Schezwan chutney
  9. Prepare 2 tbsp oil
Instructions to make Tomato Pasta:
  1. Chop the tomatoes
  2. Chop the onions
  3. Now chop the capsicum
  4. Boil the pasta for 10 minutes by adding a bit of oil and salt. Strain the watee and pour cold water on it.
  5. Now In a pan add oil. Heat it up add all the chopped veggies.
  6. Now add tomato sauce schezwan chutney and red chilli powder and salt and mix it well. Stir it for 3-4 minutes.
  7. Tomato pasta is ready to eat.

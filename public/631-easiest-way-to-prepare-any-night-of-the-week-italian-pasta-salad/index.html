The ingredients needed to make Italian Pasta Salad:
  1. Make ready 1 lb pasta of choice, I used spirals
  2. Get Kalamata olives, chopped
  3. Take Mini pepperoni
  4. Make ready Artichoke hearts, chopped
  5. Get Cherry tomatoes, halved
  6. Make ready Shredded cheese of choice
  7. Take Salt, pepper, onion and garlic powder, Italian seasoning(optional)
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup mayo
  9. Get 1/3-1/2 cup Italian dressing
Steps to make Italian Pasta Salad:
  1. Boil your pasta in salted water. Drain. Mix all ingredients together and refrigerate.

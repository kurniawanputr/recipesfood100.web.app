  1. Boil the macaroni till it is soft.
  2. Heat the fan at the oil then add onion and fry till golden brown then add magic masala black pepper salt and tomato chilli and soya sauce mix well.
  3. Add macaroni and chopped vegetables and cook for 5 minutes delicious pasta is ready to serve.

The ingredients needed to make Tangy Pasta:
  1. Make ready 250 gms Pasta
  2. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  3. Get 1 tsp refined oil
  4. Take 3 big sized Tomatoes grated
  5. Make ready 3-4 cloves garlic chopped
  6. Make ready 1 medium sized onion chopped
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp black pepper
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp Red chilli powder
  9. Take 1 tsp salt
  10. Take 1/2 tsp oregano
  11. Make ready 2 tsp oil
  12. Prepare 3 Glasses water for boiling
Instructions to make Tangy Pasta:
  1. Wash.and heat water add pasta,salt and oil. Cook.80%cook pasta.
  2. Drain.wash normal water.
  3. Heat a vok.heat oil and add garlic saute.add onion saute till transperate.
  4. Add tomatoes.mix well.cook became cook.
  5. Add salt, oregano, black pepper, red chilli, pasta.
  6. Mix well.

Steps to make Chicken Pasta:
  1. First boil the pasta. Add some oil in it.
  2. Drain the water out.
  3. Cut the onion, tomato, green chilli, chicken into small pieces.
  4. Take a pan. Heat oil. Put the pieces of onion, tomato, chicken, ginger-garlic paste.
  5. Add the boiled pasta. Add salt, sugar, black pepper powder.
  6. Add milk in it.
  7. When it is soaked…put the gas off.
  8. Add the butter cube. Now it is ready to serve.

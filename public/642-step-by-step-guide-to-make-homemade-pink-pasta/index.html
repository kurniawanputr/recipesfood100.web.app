The ingredients needed to make Pink Pasta:
  1. Make ready 1 cup pasta
  2. Get 1/2 carrot
  3. Take 1/2 potatoes
  4. Get 1 onion
  5. Make ready 4 cloves garlic
  6. Get 1/2 cup cheese
  7. Take 1 packet white pasta sauce
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp pepper powder
  9. Get 1/2 bowl grated beetroot
  10. Make ready 1 cup milk
  11. Get as per taste Salt
  12. Take as required Oil for cooking
Instructions to make Pink Pasta:
  1. Boil the pasta and keep it ready.
  2. In a pan, add oil. Once it heated then add chopped onion and garlic. Fry them for a few minutes.
  3. Now add all the veggies. Add salt as per taste, then add milk and let it boil.
  4. Once all cooked properly then add boil pasta and cheese. Mix well and add pepper powder. Once cheese melts, take out the pasta in a bowl and enjoy with family and friends.

The ingredients needed to make Creamy pasta:
  1. Take half packet pasta
  2. Make ready 1 packet cream
  3. Make ready half capsicum chopped
  4. Take half carrot chopped
  5. Get 3 pieces chicken boil
  6. Get 1 tbsp black pepper
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp white pepper
  8. Get 1 tbsp chicken powder
  9. Get 1 spoon black vinegar
  10. Make ready oil little for fry

Steps to make Creamy pasta:
  1. Take a pan fry capsicum and carrot
  2. When little tender add chicken pieces
  3. Fry well add all spices in it mix well add vinegar in it mix well
  4. Take another pan add half boil pasta in it add this mixture in it
  5. Mix well completely add cream in it mix well
  6. Put it in a full low flame for 6 min yummy pasta ready to serve

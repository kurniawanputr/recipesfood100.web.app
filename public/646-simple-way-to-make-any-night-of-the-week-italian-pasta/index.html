The ingredients needed to make Italian pasta:
  1. Get 250 gm readymade pasta
  2. Get 1 small onion
  3. Make ready 1 small tomato
  4. Get 1 small shimla mirch(capsicum)
  5. Take 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  6. Take 1 cup red sauce homemade
  7. Take 1 packet magic masala
  8. Take 2 tsp oil
  9. Make ready 1 tsp Oregano
  10. Get 1 tsp Chilli flakes
  11. Take 1 tsp Sechzwan fried masala
  12. Get to taste Salt and sugar
  13. Take 2 tsp coriander leaves

Instructions to make Italian pasta:
  1. Boil pasta
  2. Cut the vegetables in long strips
  3. To make red sauce take I small onion 2 green chilli 1 small tomato, 2 to 3 pods of garlic and small piece of ginger heat all the ingredien and crush it in mixture
  4. Now take a pan and put oil
  5. Add red sauce and mix the vegetable and fry for 5 minutes
  6. Now put pasta and mix it with the gravy.
  7. Now garnish with coriander leaves and ready to eat.

The ingredients needed to make Pasta namkeen:
  1. Prepare 250 gm Pasta
  2. Get to taste Salt
  3. Prepare as needed Oil for fry
  4. Get 1 spoon Red chilli powder
  5. Make ready 1 spoon Dry mango powder

Steps to make Pasta namkeen:
  1. Boiled pasta with salt and 1 spoon oil 90 %.
  2. Drain it off. And spread in a plate to let it cool down
  3. Now heat the oil in a kadhai and deep fry the pasta until it will crispy.
  4. Take out from the oil and sprinkle dry mango powder and red chilli powder as per your taste. Pasta namkeen is ready.

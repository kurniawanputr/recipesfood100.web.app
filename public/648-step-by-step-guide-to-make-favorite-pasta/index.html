The ingredients needed to make Pasta:
  1. Prepare 1 cup pasta
  2. Get 1/2 cup spring onion
  3. Prepare as per taste Salt
  4. Take 1 tsp red chilli powder
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp red chilli sauce
  6. Get 2 tbsp Tomato ketchup
  7. Make ready 2 tbsp oil
  8. Get 1/4 cup cabbage
  9. Take 2 finely chopped green chillies
  10. Make ready 1 sachet Maggi masala

Steps to make Pasta:
  1. Take a pan add water, salt and oil then add pasta let it boil till pasta got soften.
  2. Drain the water and take out pasta in another bowl.
  3. Take a pan add oil then add green chillies and spring onion. Saute it on medium flame till onion got transparent.
  4. Add cabbage and saute. Now add chilli sauce, tomato ketchup salt, Maggie masala and red chilli powder mix it.
  5. And boiled pasta into pan and mix it properly. Pasta is ready to serve.

The ingredients needed to make Italian pasta:
  1. Make ready 200 gm Boiled pasta
  2. Prepare 3 tbsp Oil
  3. Prepare 5 Black olives
  4. Take 2 tsp Mix herbs
  5. Make ready as per taste Black pepper powder
  6. Take 1 tsp Chilli flakes or chilli
  7. Make ready 5 tsp Mozzarella cheese
  8. Make ready For red sauce:-
  9. Prepare 4 Tomatoes
  10. Take 2 tsp Ginger, garlic and green chilli paste
  11. Get 1/2 tsp Oregano
  12. Get 1 bowl Onion chopped
  13. Prepare 2 tbsp Tomato ketchup
  14. Take 2 tbsp Green chilli sauce
  15. Take as per taste salt

Steps to make Italian pasta:
  1. Firstly take a pan and take water as required and boil it with 1/2 teaspoon of salt and 2 tbsp of cooking oil.
  2. Once the water hot enough add 200 gm of pasta, and keep the temperature on medium high,Once the water is boiled take a scissor and cut a pasta and if get cuts smoothly then your pasta boiled..Now drain the pasta and remove the excess water and keep the pasta aside..
  3. Take tomatoes cut the edges.Take a pan add water then drop tomatoes in the water. Once the tomatoes are boiled drain them and slowly remove the skin.
  4. Now blend the tomatoes till it turns into liquid.
  5. Heat pan add 2 tbsp cooking oil then add 1 tbsp ginger,garlic and green chilli paste than add chopped onion when onion colour change than add tomato mix well 2 minute.
  6. Add all herbs,all sauces,black pepper powder, red chilli flakes, oregano, black olive mix well 5 minute.
  7. Mix well and add boiled pasta and give it a nice mix.
  8. Serve them on plate,Spread some cheese on top and some coriander and mint leaves(its optional) on top just to give it a nice look.
  9. Yummy and tasty pasta is ready to serve.

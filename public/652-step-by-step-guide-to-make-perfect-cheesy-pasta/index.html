The ingredients needed to make Cheesy Pasta:
  1. Make ready 250 grams pasta
  2. Get 4 big size tomatoes
  3. Prepare 2 packet cube cheese
  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon chili flakes
  5. Get 2 tablespoons tomato sauce
  6. Take 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  7. Take 1 teaspoon sugar
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon oregano
  9. Get 2 tablespoons green coriander leaves
  10. Take 3 tbsp butter
  11. Prepare 4 tbsp cream
  12. Take as per taste Salt
Steps to make Cheesy Pasta:
  1. First of all boil the pasta and take it out in the Sieve
  2. Blanch the tomatoes and cut it in pieces
  3. In a mixture jar, grind the blanched tomatoes
  4. Now heat the pan on flame added with butter
  5. When butter melts add tomato pury
  6. Cook it for sometime then add sugar,tomato sauce, soya sauce and salt
  7. Mix all the ingredients properly cook it for 5 minutes
  8. Now add cream
  9. Mix it properly,and add boiled pasta
  10. Stire and mix all the ingredients properly for 5 minutes
  11. Switch off the flame and serve it with coriander leaves and cheese
  12. And here's Italian cheesy pasta is ready

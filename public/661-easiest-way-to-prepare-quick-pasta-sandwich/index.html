The ingredients needed to make Pasta Sandwich:
  1. Take 1 cup boiled pasta
  2. Take 1 tsp butter
  3. Prepare 1 tsp ginger garlic paste
  4. Take as per taste salt
  5. Get 1 tsp red chilli powder
  6. Take 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  7. Make ready 8 bread slice
  8. Take 4 cheese slice
  9. Prepare butter to cook
  10. Make ready 1/2 chopped capsicum
  11. Make ready 1/2 chopped carrot
  12. Take as per need few corn kernels
Steps to make Pasta Sandwich:
  1. Heat a pan add butter, ginger garlic paste and cook
  2. Add all the veggies and cook on high flame
  3. Then add boiled pasta and all the spices, ketchup and mix well
  4. Let it cool.keep aside
  5. Take a bread slice spread pasta on it & put one cheese slice and cover with another bread.
  6. Put in griller and toast it
  7. Grate cheese or put cheese slice and serve with ketchup or chutney.
  8. Use veggies of your own choice*

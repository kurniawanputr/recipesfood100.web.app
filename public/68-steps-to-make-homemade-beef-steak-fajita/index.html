The ingredients needed to make Beef Steak Fajita:
  1. Prepare Ingredients
  2. Take 1 Large Green Bell Pepper
  3. Make ready 4-5 Yellow and Red Sweet Peppers
  4. Take 1 Onion
  5. Get Skirt Steak
  6. Make ready Fajita Seasoning
  7. Make ready Oil
Steps to make Beef Steak Fajita:
  1. Marinate Steak in fajita seasoning and refrigerate for 24 hours
  2. Cut vegetables and add salt, pepper and lemon
  3. On a cast iron skillet add oil
  4. Put the steak on it
  5. Cook from both sides then slice
  6. Sauté vegetables
  7. Warm up the tortillas
  8. Layer vegetables, steak and salsa on it

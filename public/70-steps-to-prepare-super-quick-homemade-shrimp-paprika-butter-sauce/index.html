The ingredients needed to make Shrimp paprika butter sauce:
  1. Prepare 450 gr raw shrimp
  2. Get 3 tbsp butter
  3. Prepare 1 paprika
  4. Get 1/2 onion
  5. Make ready 3 cloves garlic
  6. Get 4 tbsp oyster sauce
  7. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper

The ingredients needed to make Bbq roasted beef:
  1. Make ready 3 kg beef top blade
  2. Take 1 cup pineapple chunks
  3. Prepare 5 cloves garlic
  4. Prepare 3 medium size onions
  5. Make ready 1 cup tomato sauce
  6. Make ready 1/4 cup apple cider vinegar
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup brown sugar
  8. Take 1/2 tbsp crushed pepper
  9. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper

Steps to make Bbq roasted beef:
  1. Season beef with salt and pepper and brown the outside with little amount of oil
  2. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix all together
  3. Place beef in a baking pan and cover it with the mixture made
  4. Cover with foil and bake in a 250c
  5. After an hour remove the foil and lower the temp to 150c and let it cook for an hour

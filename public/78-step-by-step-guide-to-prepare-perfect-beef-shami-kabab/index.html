The ingredients needed to make #Beef_shami_kabab:
  1. Get 1 kg Beef
  2. Get Gram lentils ½ kg
  3. Prepare 2 tsp Whole all spice
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp Red chilli powder
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp Salt
  6. Take 2 tbsp Ginger garlic paste
  7. Make ready Talhar chilli 5 (whole)
  8. Get 1 tbsp Cumin seeds
  9. Make ready Onions 3 chopped (medium)
  10. Make ready Green chillies 8 chopped
  11. Take Fresh coriander 3 bunches chopped
  12. Take Egg 3 (beaten)
  13. Make ready 1 cup Oil
  14. Prepare Water as required
Instructions to make #Beef_shami_kabab:
  1. Step 1.. - Take the beef, gram lentils, whole all spice, salt and 4 cups of water and bring it to boil. - Now add talhar chilli, red chilli powder, ginger garlic paste, cumin seeds and half of the onions. - Cook on low heat until the meat and lentils are tender. - If there is any excess water, dry it by turning the heat up
  2. Step 2.. - Take the cooked mixture in a stone mortar and pound till it becomes smooth.
  3. Step 3.. - Mix in the rest of the onions, green chillies and fresh coriander. - Take a hand full of the ground mixture and shape it into a small round patties. - Do the same for the rest of the mixture.
  4. Step 4.. - Lightly dip each patty into the beaten eggs.
  5. Step 5.. - Fry to a deep golden brown.
  6. Step 5.. - Fry to a deep golden brown.

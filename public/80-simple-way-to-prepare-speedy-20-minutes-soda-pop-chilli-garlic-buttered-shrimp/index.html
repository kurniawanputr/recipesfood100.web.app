The ingredients needed to make 20 minutes Soda Pop Chilli Garlic Buttered Shrimp:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup Sprite Soda
  2. Make ready 5 cloves garlic chopped
  3. Get Dried red chilli
  4. Make ready 1 tsp black ground pepper
  5. Get 4 sticks butter
  6. Make ready 1 kilo Shrimp
  7. Prepare 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
Instructions to make 20 minutes Soda Pop Chilli Garlic Buttered Shrimp:
  1. Melt the 2 sticks of butter in skillet over medium fire. Add the shrimp, pepper oysters sauce. Cook the shrimp for about 2-3 minutes or until both sides turned pink.
  2. Remove the shrimp and set it aside once cooked. Then on the same pan, add the garlic and chilli stir and then add the 1/2 cup of Sprite Soda pop. Boil and let it simmer for about 2 minutes to reduce the sauce.
  3. Add the remaining butter, melt and then add the shrimp. Stir for 1-2 minutes gently then serve hot!

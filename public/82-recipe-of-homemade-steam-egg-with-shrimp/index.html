The ingredients needed to make Steam Egg with Shrimp:
  1. Prepare 2 eggs
  2. Make ready to taste cooked shrimp, cut into small cubes
  3. Prepare 180 ml water
  4. Prepare to taste salt
  5. Take to taste white pepper
Steps to make Steam Egg with Shrimp:
  1. Boil water into a pot.
  2. Mix all the ingredients until properly combined. Place on a steamer rack. And steam for 15 mins. In a very low fire.

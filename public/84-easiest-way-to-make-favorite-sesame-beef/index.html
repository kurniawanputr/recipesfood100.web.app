The ingredients needed to make Sesame Beef:
  1. Get Beef (sliced)
  2. Make ready 1,5 tablespoon sesame oil
  3. Prepare 3 cloves garlic, minced
  4. Prepare Water
  5. Make ready Onion, cut into 2 inch lenght
  6. Prepare White sesame seeds for garnishing
  7. Prepare To taste beef powder
  8. Make ready To taste black pepper
  9. Get Marinade
  10. Make ready 2 tablespoon dark soy sauce
  11. Prepare 2 table spoon light soy sauce
  12. Prepare 1 teaspoon cornstarch
  13. Prepare 1 tablespoon rice cooking wine
  14. Get 2 teaspoon sugar
  15. Take 1 tablespoon ginger juice
Instructions to make Sesame Beef:
  1. Mix all marinade ingredients,
  2. Marinade the beef with mixed marinade ingredients. Set aside for 20 minutes
  3. Heat up a pan or wok, add sesame oil, put in onion fry until light brown add garlic stri fry untill they are fragrant. Put in beef stir fry for few minute add some water, stir again, add black pepper, beef powder, add some sesame oil and this is it :)
  4. It is ready to serve, place it on the plate and garnish with white sesame seeds, nom nom :)

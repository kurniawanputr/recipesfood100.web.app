The ingredients needed to make Beef Tongseng:
  1. Get 400 gr brisket, cut into bite size
  2. Take 6 small tomatoes or 2 medium tomatoes
  3. Get 5 cabbage leaves, roughly cut some bird eye chili peppers (to taste-optional)
  4. Take 2 green onions, cut 1 inch
  5. Prepare 1 Tbsp curry powder
  6. Prepare 2 lemongrass, bruised
  7. Make ready 3-4 kaffir lime leaves
  8. Prepare 2 Tbsp vegetable oil
  9. Prepare 350 ml water
  10. Get 1 Tbsp salt or to taste
  11. Prepare 1/2 tsp white pepper powder or to taste
  12. Prepare 1/2 tsp sugar
  13. Get 1/2 tsp beef bouillon
  14. Prepare 2-3 Tbsp sweet soy sauce
  15. Make ready fried shallots for garnish
  16. Make ready Ground spices:
  17. Get 4 cloves garlic
  18. Prepare 6 cloves shallots
  19. Take 6 red chili peppers
  20. Make ready 3 bird eye chili peppers (optional)
  21. Prepare 1 inch ginger
  22. Get 3 nutmeg
Steps to make Beef Tongseng:
  1. Heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat. SautÃ© ground spices, lemongrass, and kaffir lime leaves until fragrant. - Add curry powder. Mix well.
  2. Add beef. Cook until the meat change color. Remove from the heat.
  3. Place the meat in a slow cooker. Add water, salt, sugar, white pepper powder, and beef bouillon. Cover the pot. Cook on high for 4 hours.
  4. Add cabbage, tomatoes, green onions, bird eye chili peppers (if you use some), and sweet soy sauce. Cook again for 15 minutes. Remove the pot.

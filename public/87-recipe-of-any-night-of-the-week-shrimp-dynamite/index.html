The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Dynamite:
  1. Take Marinate shrimp
  2. Get 250 g Shrimp
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp Paprika powder
  4. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  5. Make ready 1/4 tsp Red chilli
  6. Take 1/4 tsp garlic powder
  7. Get 1 tbsp Soya sauce
  8. Get 1 egg
  9. Get Oil for frying
  10. Prepare For coating
  11. Take 1/2 cup purpose flour
  12. Prepare 1/4 cup corn flour
  13. Take Pinch salt
  14. Prepare For Sauce
  15. Make ready 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  16. Prepare 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  17. Get 2 tbsp chili sauce
  18. Get 2 tsp honey
  19. Prepare For garnish
  20. Get Sesame seed
  21. Make ready Lettuce
  22. Get Spring onion
Steps to make Shrimp Dynamite:
  1. Shrimp main Paprika powder salt Red chilli ko add karein
  2. Phir Soya sauce or egg add karein
  3. In ko achi tarha mix karein
  4. Phir dosrey bowl main purpose flour corn flour or pinch of salt add karein or mix karein
  5. Phir shrimp ko flour Jo mix Kia tha is mein fold karein
  6. Phir oil ko gram karein medium flame pe
  7. Phir shrimp ko deep fry karein
  8. Crispy fry or golden ho tu nikal dain
  9. Phir aik bowl main mayonnaise ketchup daley
  10. Chili sauce or honey add karein or mix karein
  11. Phir shrimp ko sauce waley better main fold karein
  12. Phir serving bowl main transfer karein or enjoy karein

The ingredients needed to make Shrimp Fried Rice:
  1. Prepare 2 Tbsp vegetable oil
  2. Make ready 2 plates cooked white rice (leftover rice, put in the refrigerator for a night, is recommended)
  3. Take 250 gr shrimps, peeled and deveined
  4. Take 50 gr frozen peas
  5. Get 50 gr frozen corn
  6. Get 1 medium carrot, small diced
  7. Make ready 2 cloves garlic, minced
  8. Make ready 2 Tbsp soy sauce
  9. Get 1/2 tsp salt or to taste
  10. Get 1/4 tsp white pepper powder or to taste
  11. Prepare 2 stalks green onions, thinly sliced
  12. Get 1/2 Tbsp sesame oil
Steps to make Shrimp Fried Rice:

  2. Add shrimps and cook until the shrimps change color.
  3. Add rice. Then season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper. Test the taste.

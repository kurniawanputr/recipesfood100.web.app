The ingredients needed to make Chicken & shrimp Alfredo:
  1. Prepare 1 bag spinach
  2. Get 3 chicken breast cut into strips
  3. Make ready 1 bag Raw shrimp
  4. Take 4 Cups Heavy Whipping Cream
  5. Prepare 6 Cloves Garlic
  6. Get Creole Seasoning
  7. Make ready Old Bay Garlic&Herb Seasoning
  8. Take 1 contain of Fresh Grated Parmesan/ramano Cheese
  9. Take Olive oil
  10. Get 1 box penne pasta noodles
  11. Prepare 2 stick butter
  12. Prepare Parsley

Instructions to make Chicken & shrimp Alfredo:
  1. Saute or Bake Chicken pieces & season with old bay and creole seasoning
  2. Add Olive oil and 1 clove of chopped garlic to a pan, Then Saute Spinach until its Wilted
  3. Drain Spinach and Set Aside Until Later
  4. Add olive oil and 1 clove of chopped garlic to a pan and add raw shrimp cookies for 2 minutes on each side, Set aside for Later
  5. Start boiling pasta for About 10-12 minutes
  6. For the Sauce: Chop remaining garlic and sauce in a pan with butter.
  7. Once butter is melted. Add Heavy whipping cream and stir. Then Add That Cheese and Stir and Until Completely melted.
  8. Add creole and old bay seasoning. Simmer the sauce on low for 5 minutes.
  9. Add all remaining ingredients to the cooked pasta then pour the sauce on top and Garnish with Parsley.

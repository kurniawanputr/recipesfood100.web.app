The ingredients needed to make Beef Pho:
  1. Get 3 meatballs
  2. Get 3 wagyu slices
  3. Make ready Flat rice noodle
  4. Make ready Spices
  5. Get 1/2 pack beef pho spices
  6. Get Condiments
  7. Prepare Spinach
  8. Get Mint
  9. Make ready Basil
  10. Get Corriander
  11. Take Bean sprout
  12. Get Chillies
  13. Make ready Lemon
Instructions to make Beef Pho:
  1. Boil the pho spices and let it cool overnight
  2. Prepare all condiments and flat rice noodle
  3. When about to eat, cook the beef and meatballs in the broth

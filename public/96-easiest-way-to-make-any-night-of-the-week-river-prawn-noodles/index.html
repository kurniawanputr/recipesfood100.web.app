The ingredients needed to make River Prawn Noodles 生虾伊面:
  1. Make ready River Prawn (cut into ½)
  2. Take Noodles
  3. Make ready Gingers
  4. Make ready Spring Onion
  5. Take Salt
  6. Make ready Sugar
  7. Take Fish Sauce
  8. Make ready Eggs
  9. Take Cooking Wine
Steps to make River Prawn Noodles 生虾伊面:
  1. Hot wok with 1ltr of water to cook the prawn for about 3 minutes.
  2. Remove the prawn and reused the water as stock…
  3. Add in ginger into the stock to boil then follow by the taste I.e salt, sugar & fish sauce
  4. Once the taste accepted, add in the noodles for about 2~3minutes
  5. Add in the prawn, cooking wine & spring onion.. ready to be served.

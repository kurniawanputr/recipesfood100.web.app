The ingredients needed to make Grilled Citrus herb shrimp:
  1. Get 1-2 lbs. Raw shrimp
  2. Get 1 T fresh parsley
  3. Prepare 1 T fresh rosemary
  4. Get 1 T fresh cilantro
  5. Take 1 T fresh basil
  6. Get 1 T fresh thyme
  7. Get 1-2 T orange zest
  8. Get 1/4 cup olive oil
  9. Make ready 1/4 cup orange juice
  10. Get 3 T honey
  11. Make ready 3-5 cloves garlic
  12. Prepare To taste salt and pepper
  13. Prepare Skewers
Steps to make Grilled Citrus herb shrimp:
  1. Mix all ingredients together except shrimp.
  2. Devine and take shells off shrimp.
  3. Put shrimp in a bag and marinade with sauce for 1-3 hours. No longer since marinade has citrus it will start to cook the shrimp.
  4. While marinading shrimp let the skewers soak, if using wooden skewers. (if using metal skip this step.)
  5. Take shrimp out of fridge and put about 5 or 6 shrimp on each skewer.
  6. Grill about 3-5 mintues on each side, or until shrimp turns pink.
  7. Enjoy over rice, in a tortilla, or simply right off the skewer.
